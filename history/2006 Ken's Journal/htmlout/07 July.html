Saturday, July 01, 2006

 

                I got up at 6:30 AM so that I could get a early start at removing the dead trees in back.  Mae got up and got breakfast ready for me.  She cleaned house this morning.  It was after noon before we finished and we were both very tired.  I showed Mae how to balance the bank statement and then took a nap.  When I woke she had it just about complete but was out of balance.  I worked with her and we soon found the errors.  I spent the rest of the afternoon getting the welder and other materials needed to build the trailer on our tent trailer so we can pull the boat behind it.  Mae and I watched some recorded shows before going to bed.

 

Sunday, July 02, 2006

 

                We slept until about 7 when I got up.  I needed to get our tithing ready so while I had Quicken up I made some entries into it.  Meetings were very good and I enjoyed every one of them.  I was able to stay awake through all the block.  I helped Mae start dinner and then I took a nap for ½ hr.  We had Troy, Meliah, Weston, Makayla, Scott, Mishelle, Kylan and Ashley all over for dinner.  After dinner I got the scooter out and they had fun riding that.  Sandi and her two granddaughters came over for a few minutes to show us some pictures that Mike had taken of Sandi and Nick.  She also talked to Mae about printing off some labels for her.  When everyone left we were very tired.  I took the garbage out and then I packaged some meat for Mae, in the seal-a-meal.  I filled out my journal and we went to bed.  Mae cleaned up some of the mess first.

 

Monday, July 03, 2006

 

                I got up at 5 AM so I could get an early start on the camp trailer.  I had bought the wrong part to fix a 220 V extension cord so I had to go to Home Depot and exchange it.  It was after 8 AM before I really got started.  At noon Irven and his family came and we went to Willard Bay boating.  Irven, Sandy, Andrea, Jennifer, Chris, Mark, Mae and I all went.  We had a lot of fun but it was late when we got back home.  I went over to the store and picked up some items for Mae.  I filled out my journal while Mae was getting things ready for tomorrow.

 

Tuesday, July 04, 2006

 

                I got up at 5:30 AM but didn’t feel very good so I went back to bed.  Mae and I prepared breakfast for our family.  All the boys and their families came for breakfast.  We prepared biscuits, gravy, eggs and fish.   We then spent the day visiting and playing games.  For dinner we had salads and grilled meats.  Bob, Mark and Irven joined us for dinner.  It started to blow and then rain.  David was concerned about his place and this storm so they left about 5 PM.  Mike and his family went up to David’s for the evening.  Mae played games with her grandchildren while we watched a show on TV.  Mae went over to Layton to watch the fire-works display with Scott and his family.  I stayed home and watched a John Wayne movie. 

 

Wednesday, July 05, 2006

 

                We didn’t get to bed until almost 2 AM so we slept until 6 AM.  Mae and I took the Buick over to
Western Radiator to have them clean out the radiator and change the anti-freeze.  I spent most of the day working on the tent trailer getting the hitch built on the back.  I took Bob over to get his car from the garage and then picked up some parts.  This evening Mae cleaned out the boat while I did some other work and got tools put away and put what tools I want with me in the truck.  It is 10 PM and I am tired so I will finish this entry and take a shower and go to bed.  I have a back upper left tooth that is bothering me this evening.  I had to take some pain pills in order to sleep.

 

Thursday, July 06, 2006

 

                I got up early but still had a bad tooth ache so we called Dr. Cottle and was able to get in at 10:30 AM.  I got the truck and trailers wired for lights before I went over to Dr. Cottle’s.  After x-raying the tooth he said it was just beginning to abases and it either needed to do a root cannel or pull it.  Being that is a upper muller I told him to pull it.  When I got home we finished packing and soon left.  With the trailer set up we could only drive 45 MPH or the boat trailer would start fish tailing.  We got to East Cannon North Camp ground and set up camp.  Sandi and Nick came about 6:30 PM.  We went out fishing for a while and caught three fish before we came in.  I was going to leave the boat on the water but it looked like storme so we took the boat our.  I had Nick back the boat trailer in and after he told me that was the first he had backed a trailer.  After eating some dinner we played our marble game.  Nick and I won both games.  We enjoyed having them with us.   It was late when we went to bed.

 

Friday, July 07, 2006

                We got up early and went out fishing.  We cooked up the fish for breakfast.  It was a beautiful morning and almost as soon as we started fishing Sandi caught a nice 16” trout.  They were slower after that but we did catch a couple more before we came in.  Mike and Mattie came about noon and stayed with us the rest of the day.  We caught a few fish but it was slow.  Mike, Mattie, Sandi and Nick left this evening.  We checked on the showers and felt good about them so we took a shower before going to bed.

 

Saturday, July 08, 2006

 

                We thought about going fishing this morning but decided to sleep in and then prepare to leave.  It was 1 PM before we to come home.  We decided it is a big job to pack.  We stopped at the exit by David’s and he met us there so we could give him Conner’s fishing pole and Shauna’s purse.  They had been left at our place on the 4th.  David also gave us a check for the work I did for them.  We got home at 3 PM and the Clarks came at 3:30 PM for us to go with them.  The Maharry’s, the Hyer’s, the Clark’s and us met at Maddock’s for dinner.  We enjoyed visiting with all of them and enjoyed riding with the Clark’s.  We unloaded the pickup and put things away.  Jeff, Gale and Benjamin stopped for a few minutes.  I caught my journal up to date and we went to bed very tired.

 

 

Sunday, July 09, 2006

 

                I got up early but went back to bed and slept until after 8 AM.  Mae woke me to let me know that Bob would have breakfast ready in a few minutes.  He had prepared biscuits and gravy for us.  I did get most of the Priesthood lesson read before we went to church.  Sandy came over and went to church with us.  She had taken Irven back to the hospital yesterday.  This is sure a trial for them and it is hard to even suggest what they should do.  Sandy stayed for dinner as Bob and Carroll invited us to eat with them.  Bob prepared a wonderful roast dinner for us.  It is no wonder that I am gaining wait.  Mae and I went over to Scott and Mishelle’s at 5 PM.  I called Kip Michaelis to see if he was finished with my staple gun.  He had, so I went over and got it.  Mishelle had made Navaho Tacos for dinner and we ate with them.  Having just eaten one was plenty for us.  Mae and I stayed with Kylan and Ashley while Scott and Mishelle went to a meeting about the youth pioneer trek for their Stake.  We finished reading a chapter in our book before we came in, so it was 11 PM when we got in the house.  Bob came down with a plate of cookies he had baked with raw sugar so I could eat them.  They were very good but I only ate one this late at night.  I filled out my journal and we went to bed about mid-night.

 

 

Monday, July 10, 2006

 

                I got up at 4:30 AM and got dressed.  I went upstairs but Mark was still asleep so I went down to the Stake onion patch by myself.  I got there at 5:30 AM and walked down the three rows assigned to the High Priest Group.  They were in good shape but the rows assigned to the Elders were in pretty bad shape.  I started weeding one row as I worked my way back.  Mark came at 6:30 AM and helped me with the row I was on.  We finished that row at 9:30 AM and came home.  After eating breakfast I tried to call ACE Hardware in Alpine Wyo. but all sales representatives were busy.  I took a nap for 1 ½ hr.  When I got up I again called the ACE Hardware and talked to Don.  He had me fax him the plans and he got back to me with the prices.  Mae and I went out to Grantsville and tried to help Sandi.  Sandi had received the pictures back that Mike had taken last Saturday.  They turned out very good.  I couldn’t help feeling that maybe Sandi is getting the marriage that she should have had several years ago, I sure hope so.  Sandi gave me most of Terry’s fishing equipment; Terry’s boys didn’t want them.  We went up to the Deseret Peak Complex 7 PM to watched Nick and the team he is on play a game, they won 18 to 8.  We then came on home getting here about 10 PM.  The ride out and back gave us some time to read from our book.  We went to bed with concerns and tears in our eyes.  When we left to serve as missionaries all six of our children went to the Temple with us.  Now, three of the six are not worthy to attend the Temple at this time.  We do not know just how Shellie and Roy are doing and are afraid to ask.  Earlier this year Shellie expressed great concerns and when we responded with suggestions she got up set with us.  Mike is criticizing Sandi for getting married and giving up the government retirement that Terry left her.  Sandi said that she and Nick have talked of just living together but decided that because of Chelci, Mae and I and the talk that would take place that they wouldn’t.  I wonder where we went wrong in teaching our family.  Scott is also looking for work as the company he has been working for gave them their last check last week.  He is now looking for work.

 

Tuesday, July 11, 2006

 

                We got up early and went to the Temple this morning, feeling we needed a spiritual lift.  We did enjoy the session and were ask to be the company witnesses.  Mae took me home after the temple session and then she went to Farmington to walk with Jackie and have lunch.  I took a ½ hour nap and then tried to work on Elise’s project.  Mae called and is having car problems.  I rented a car caddy and towed it over to Western Radiator.  We left it there for them to check it out.  I changed clothes when we got home and Bob and I went Home Teaching at the Johnston’s.  Mae and I then went up to Morgan to watch Tyler’s ball game.  This is the last game of their season.  It was fun to watch the game.  We come on home and I made my journal entry before we went to bed.

 

Wednesday, July 12, 2006
