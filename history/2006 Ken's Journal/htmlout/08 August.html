                I got up at 3 AM and was at Randy’s by 5 AM.  We went up to Strawberry Reservoir and went fishing.  When we launched my boat and tried to start it the battery was to dead.  I removed the battery from my pickup and carried it to the boat and used it to jump the boat.  It started right off.  We fished all day catching many fish but were only able to keep 4 of what we caught.  The rest were over 15 inches but were under 22 inches.  I do not agree with this limit as to many fish are injured and will die.  It was after 7 PM when I got home.  Mae fixed some dinner and I bathed before going to bed.  My stomach didn’t feel to good and Mae still wanted to stay up for a while so I went to sleep in the recliner.  I woke a little latter with stomach cramps and diarrhea.  I slept the rest of the night in the recliner.

 

Thursday, August 31, 2006

 

                I woke before the alarm went off so I turned it off and went back to bed, but neither of us went back to sleep so we decided to go fishing anyway.  We didn’t leave until about 8 am.  We went to East Canyon Reservoir.  We didn’t catch any fish and only had two bites, but we enjoyed the day anyway.  It was a beautiful day and the lake and scenery were beautiful.  Mae read quite a lot in our book series “Prelude to Glory”.  This afternoon about 4 PM we decided to give up on fishing.  We set the trolling plate up so we could speed up and just went boating.  I am very pleased with the performance of the boat.  On the way home I asked Mae if she would like to stop at the restaurant in Morgan for supper.  We parked the vehicles and ate a good chicken dinner.  Robin and her younger children came to visit right when we got home.  I fell asleep setting in the recliner.  I was to tired to make this entry tonight so went to bed.
