Sunday, June 01, 2008

 

                I woke and got up at 6:45AM and read my scriptures meditating about Faith, Hope and Charity along with the Blessing of Rachael Lynn today.  How I pray that I can be lead in the prayer that I give.  My Fasting today will sure be along those desires.  David’s family will not be there as they are out of town.  I gathered from Tyler that this is a fishing trip that they will be to.  I fear that it is an excuse so that David will not be present when his other two brothers join the circle for the Blessing.  Oh how wish, hope and pray that some how David and Jeff can somehow be touched. 

                The weather was so nice that we walked to our church house.  We attended our meetings and enjoyed them.  After getting home I went outside and fed the rabbits.  The white and brown rabbit is pulling fur and making a nest.  She should have kits before long.  I don’t think the black rabbit took.  When I came back I noticed that the dog had dug in the flower bed and made quite a mess.  It really upset me and I do not like the feelings I have about the dog and the attitude that Carroll seems to have about it.  After cleaning up the mess I came inside and tried to take a nap.  I rested but was so upset over the dog that I didn’t sleep.

Saturday, June 28, 2008

 

                I woke about 4:20AM and went into the front room and slept the rest of the night in the recliner.  Mae made French toast and heated up left-over fish from yesterday.  I took care of the rabbits and then chopped the hay I had wind rowed to dry last week.  I started on digging a trench to add another sprinkler to the yard.  Bob prepared liver and onions for lunch and of course cooked some chicken for Mae.  After lunch I took a nap and then finished adding the sprinkler head.  We hope that it will cover a area that is turning brown.  I had Bob mow some of the back while I put the trailer away.  I also started up the snowmobiles and warmed them up.  I added a gas additive to both machines to help keep the gas.  Mae and I took showers and went to a production at the Roy High School of The White Star.  Rick and Georgia’s granddaughter, Morgan Tew, was one of the actors in the play.  She did very well and we enjoyed the production.  It was late when we got home.  I filled in this entry before we went to bed.  I was very tired and hurting when we went to bed.

 

Sunday, June 29, 2008

 

                I got up at 7AM but was still very tired.  I got dressed, went out and fed the rabbits and then went over to the church and helped set up chairs.  My legs were hurting quite badly when I came back home so before going to Church I took some Tylenol.  Being it is the fifth Sunday the Priesthood met with the Relief Society for a special lesson.  About half was through I begin to really hurt.  After the class closed I came home and went back to bed.  Mae woke me at 12:30PM for dinner.  I feel some better but my legs still hurt.  After dinner Mae lay down and took a nap.  I worked on my journal and history for quite a while today.  Mae and I took time to go to the Johnston’s to see how they are feeling.  Mae said we should keep it short but when we left over an hour later I told her that she can now see why Bob and I are so long when we visit them.  They are wonderful people and I do enjoy visiting with them.  I think Sister Johnston enjoyed having Mae there so they could visit while Patriarch Johnston and I reminisced.  Time went by quickly and it was after 10PM before we went to bed.

 

Monday, June 30, 2008

 

                The alarm went off at 6AM but we slept in a little longer.  Shauna brought the children at 8AM for Mae to tend today.  we were just eating breakfast.  We took the children out and I showed them the baby rabbit and let them pet the others.  Mae worked with the children and had then weed some of the garden.  Mae took the children to the Hill Aerospace Museum.  They really had a good time there.  I changed front brake pads on Bob’s car for him.  I got on the inter-net and on www.ksl.com found a lady in Ephraim that is selling New Zealand White rabbits.  She will bring me a buck and two does on Saturday the 5th of July.  I will have to go to Draper to get them from her.  Now I will have to build more pens.  I did some searching for Down Riggers for my boat.  Bob and I went to Smith & Edward’s and looked at the Down Riggers they have.  I would like to buy an electric operated one but they are about $500 for one and the manual would cost less than ½ of the electric.  I decided that I would hold off and think about it.  Sandi called me and said that Nick would pay for one downrigger.  We are considering just installing one as I found that you can use two poles on one downrigger.  We were late getting to bed this evening.  we watched a Doris Day movie before going to sleep.  The Comcast channel would not work so we had to call in and they will send a repairman out tomorrow.
