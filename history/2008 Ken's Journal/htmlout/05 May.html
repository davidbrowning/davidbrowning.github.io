                I didn’t sleep very well last night and spent most of the night in the recliner.  I moved our tent trailer out of the covering but found that the trailer had a flat tire.  I removed the tire and had Bob take it to Big O’s before he went over to see Carroll.  Mark and I went to Sister Olsen’s and started removing some trees.  Bishop Knudsen came over and helped us with the project.  We quite at noon for lunch and I went home and ate dinner with Scott and Mae.  Jeff was busy at work and couldn’t take the time for lunch.  After lunch Scott had left I took a nap and then went back on the project.  We worked until after 4PM and then Mark and I stacked the wood that we had cut up into firewood.  Mae cleaned Bob and Carroll’s house today, trimmed the yard, fixed lunch, went over and got my chain saw from the repair shop, and was blowing the side walks of when I came home.  I suggested that we call for a home delivery for dinner but Mae had it about ready.  We turned on the TV and watched the last of a Texas Ranger show and then watched a Mystery Woman series.  Mae fell asleep before it finished.  I entered this entry and took a shower before we went to bed.

 

Thursday, May 29, 2008

 

                I woke before 6AM and got ready to go into the bus terminal so that I could take 9th graders from Syracuse Jr. High to Lagoon.  There were 8 buses to taking all the students.  Before we left the school Roberta called me and asks if I could just drop off my load and return so that I could take another route this afternoon.  Many of the youth will stay and come back with other later this evening so we will not need 8 busses to return the students anyway.  I came home and ate lunch with Mae.  While home I did some searching on Goggle Earth for route maps.  Mae has been busy doing house work both upstairs and downstairs this morning.  She went over to the Clearfield Family History Center and got Brother Farr’s files.  She also took the tire to our camp trailer to the shop to have it repaired.  I got over to the shop at 1:37 PM for the bus routs.  I started with Northridge High and then North Layton Jr. High and finished with Mountain View Elementary.  I returned to the yard at 412 PM.  When I got home I changed clothes and helped Mark take Bob pickup up to the Dumps and unload it.  I had to hurry to change when I got home so that Mae and I could go to the viewing for Brother Farr.  We went to the Lindquist Mortuary in Clearfield but no one was there.  I guess the best we can say is that we tried.  I got the boat ready to go in the morning and then came in.  Mae got on the computer and did some on Family History.  I got this entry typed and we went to bed.

 

Friday, May 30, 2008

 

                I got up early and went fishing.  I fueled the boat and pickup, and then met Nick at the parking lot at the mouth of Weber canyon.  We went to David’s and got Tyler and then went to East Canyon reservoir.  We had very good luck and all of us caught fish.  Tyler caught the first one and also caught the largest one.  We released quite a few fish just so we could continue fishing for a while longer.  We left the lake before noon.  We stopped at the gate and I bought a season pass.  Tyler took two fish and Nick had me take the rest.  He said that we could invite then out for breakfast one of these days.  After getting home I went to Sister Fallis’s and rotovated her garden spot.  I then went to Sister Olsen’s and helped Mark, Karin and Glen load the tree branches onto a trailer and tie them down.  I was sure tired when I quite for the day.  Mae and I watched a show on Comcast this evening.  I sure love that girl.

 

Saturday, May 31, 2008

 

                I didn’t get up until 6:30AM and I let Mae sleep until after 7AM.  After eating breakfast I went over to Sister Olsen’s to help Mark and Glenn take the limbs to dump.  When I got there they had already left so I got the lawn mower and went to Sister Fallis’s and mowed her back lot.  I then mowed her lawn.  From there I mowed our lawn.  After lunch I took a nap and then went to Jeff’s mother-in-law’s and helped Jeff install some trim.  I stopped on the way home and got natural gas for my truck.  I was sure tired tonight so we had a light dinner, I then took a shower, entered this journal entry and we watched a show before going to bed.
