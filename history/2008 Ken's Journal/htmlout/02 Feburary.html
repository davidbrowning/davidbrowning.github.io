Tuesday, February 26, 2008

Wednesday, February 27, 2008

 

                I woke a little before 6AM but waited until the alarm went off,  I let Mae sleep a little longer.  We were busy getting breakfast and getting things packed to leave.  It was after 10AM when we left the house.  We stopped at Standard Optical and got our glasses.  They got us in on a good promotion but by the time we got the glasses it cost us over $300 a set.  So much for a good deal.  We picked up some items at the health store and got gas in Ogden on 21st street.  Mae read most of the way and we ate a light lunch while traveling.  It was about 1PM when we got to Lave.  After getting into our room we took a couple of mineral bath and watched a couple of shows.  Sandi and Nick got here at 10:35PM. 

 

Thursday, February 28, 2008

 

                We got up in time to set in the hot mineral tub before starting breakfast.  We cooked breakfast of sausage, hash browns and eggs and had Sandi and Nick eat with us.  We packed up and went to Soda Springs and then North but after traveling almost 10 miles stopped and asked where Trail Creek Lodge was.  It was back about 9 miles.  We had passed the road.  We found it ok when we returned and then traveled about 6 miles east to the lodge.  We were excited with the lodge.  The owners mother, Pat, made us feel so welcome, showed us our rooms and told us that the snowmobile was ready for us about 2 miles south at a warming hut.  We found everything ready and were soon on the snowmobiles.  The trail was freshly groomed and there was plenty of valleys and hills for us to play on.  This was Nick’s first time at driving a snowmobile so we started out careful.    The snowmobile that we rented was a two passenger machine and Mae road with me on it.  I decided to clime one hill so Mae got off and I went up it and came back down.  Sandi then went up and when she returned I talked Mae into riding with me.  About ½ ways up the hill, the machine tipped in some soft snow and we rolled over.  No one was hurt and we just had to work a little to get the machine turned back right side up.  I had Mae drive as we started back up the groomed trail.  Sandi took the lead and she went up on the side hill so Nick followed her.  I guess Mae figured if Nick could do it so could she.  However she let up on the throttle and the machine rolled over.  Again we had to work to get it right side up again.  The next hill we climbed Nick got stuck so I went back and helped him get it out.  Sandi go quite cocky about the fact that every one had got stuck except her.  Just a few minutes later Sandi and I had to turn the machines around and Sandi sunk her machine into the deep snow and I had to pull her out.  We got back at her for her earlier comments.  We had a great time and traveled until we had come to the Cash County line.  We then turned around and returned to the warming hut where we ate our lunch.  We decided to call it a day and loaded up the machines.  We returned to the lodge and there popped corn and played games.  We ended up watching a show that Sandi had brought.  It was fair but a few times the Lords name was used in vain.  It was after min-night when we went to bed.

 

Friday, February 29, 2008

 

                We slept in getting up in time to have breakfast about 9:30AM.  The lodge prepared us breakfast of sausage, scrambled eggs, hash brown potatoes and toast.  We also were served orange juice and jam with our toast.  After eating we packed our belonging and checked out.  The cost of staying at the lodge, renting the snowmobile which included breakfast, any winter clothing we needed and the fuel for the snowmobile was less than what we paid for the one night stay at the motel in Lava.  We took our snowmobiles to the warming hut and unloaded them.  Two of us went out on the snowmobiles while two stayed and played games in the warming hut.  Sandi and I went out first.  I had wanted Mae and Sandi to go out as I wanted to visit with Nick; however Mae suggested the other arrangement so that Sandi and I could have fun on the machines.  We did enjoy it and was out for a while.  When we came back Mae and I went out for a while.  Mae had trouble when we got off the groomed trail so she stayed on the trail and I went off into the fresh areas.  Nick and Sandi went out for a while.  Mae and I played some games while they were gone.  When they came back I got the trailer ready to load up.  Sandi then cut up the hill so I followed after her.  We played around for a little while before loading the machines.  We stopped at the lodge to be sure we had settled up and then started for home.  we stopped in Soda Springs and filled the machines with fuel and got some frozen yogurts.  We then drove to Madox’s in Brigham City where Sandi and Nick treated us to dinner for my Birthday present.  We sure enjoyed eating and visiting with Sandi and Nick.  We then traveled on towards home.  Mae and I turned off at 21st Street but Sandi and Nick went on.  We filled up with gas and natural gas although the natural gas wasn’t working very good so we only got about ½ a tank of natural gas.  It was 9PM when we got home and much later when we got to bed.
