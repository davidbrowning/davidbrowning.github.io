                David brought Conner for Mae to tend this morning.  After feeding the rabbits (the children helped me, they love to do that), I took my boat over to Dick’s Boats and had them winterize it.  After dropping it off, I went to the tool rental and rented a Rota-tiller.  It took me almost 2 hours to till the garden area.  Mae had lunch ready when I returned home.  I took a nap and then went back and got my boat.  I stopped at Macey’s and bought a few items for Mae and then went to the Good Earth Health Store and bought some food there.  Riverdale Road was really a mess with the construction.  I got home just in time to eat some dinner and then we took Kylan and Ashely over to the hospital to see their Mother and Father.  David had come earlier to pick up Connor.  Mae played games with the grandchildren & took them to the park during the day.  It was late when we got home so we put the children to bed and then cleaned up and went to bed.

 

Tuesday, October 28, 2008

 

                I got a call at 5:10 AM this morning by the bus dispatch, asking if I could make a run but I turned it down.  I helped Mae with breakfast, fed the rabbits and then we got things packed to go.  We got over to the hospital at 11AM but Jackie and Dennis were late.  We took the children to the nursery where Scott and Mishelle were able to bring the babies to the window and show them to Kylan and Ashley.  The children were thrilled.  We left Kylan and Ashley with Dennis and Jackie for them to tend.  We took Scott and Mishelle’s car to their place and left it.  We stopped at Sub-Way in Kaysville and got a salad for each of us.  it was 1:30PM before we got to Park City.  We spent the afternoon with Sandi, Nick, Brad, JaNae and their children, looking around the Condo and playing games.  Nick and I played a game of Giant Chess.  They left for home about 5PM.  We enjoyed grilling chicken breast for our dinner.  We decided to watch a DVD this evening but was unable to get the machine to run.  We called Sandi and Nick but when we still couldn’t get it to work we called the desk.  They finally sent a repairman up and when he couldn’t get it to work he turned the screen around and found that it had been hooked up wrong.  We started to watch one DVD but the language was terrible so we shut it off and watched another.  The movie was “The Man of Fire” with Densil Washington and though it was quite violent, the language was much better and the plot was good.  We sure enjoyed the evening together.

 

Wednesday, October 29, 2008

 

                We slept in and then went out looking for some place to eat.  I had my mind set on an omelet but we couldn’t find a café that was open in Park City.  We drove out of town and saw a sigh advertising a café ahead.  It was a fast food joint at a service station.  When we went to order they wouldn’t serve breakfast after 11AM.  The girl irritated me with her attitude and dress so we just left.  We tried to find a Dinner that Larry and Dian Steel told us of last Sunday.  By then it was late and I was very hungry so we stopped at a grocery store in Kamas where we bought some fried chicken and some salads and ate there.  We then bought some other groceries.  As we checked out we asked the clerk about the Dinner and she told us where it was.  We drove there just to find it and then drove on up to Smith and Morehouse reservoir and camp grounds.  We were very excited to find the area and think that maybe we will come back next summer for a few days.  We came back to the condo where I entered my journal and we took a nap.  This afternoon we went swimming, enjoyed the hot tubs, played table tennis (which Mae won), and played a couple of games of Fox and Geese, (which I won 2 out of 3).  I grilled steak while Mae got the rest of the meal ready.  After eating we watched two DVDs, “This is The Army” and “Walk the Line”.

 

Thursday, October 30, 2008

 

                We slept in and then had breakfast.  After cleaning up we went for a walk around the condominiums and ski shops and then walked downtown.  We then went hot tubing and swimming.  We then came back to the room and showered.  I took a nap while Mae did her hair.  We came down to the Ogden Temple and attended the 5:45PM Stake endowment session.  After the session we stopped at the Chuck Wagon and had dinner.  It was 10:30PM before we got back to the condo so we put on a DVD and watched that.  Mae fell asleep during the show so she didn’t see much of it.

 

Friday, October 31, 2008

 

                We slept in late and then took a bath in the double tub.  The front desk called at 11AM, to remind us that we were to check out at 10AM.  We thought that it was noon for the checkout.  We hurried and packed up.  It was funny because I packed the bath room items and Mae packed the bedroom things.  Mae wasn’t finished with the bathroom things so had to re-open those items and then I found that Mae had packed all my clothes and I wasn’t completely dressed.  We laughed about that.  We were out before noon.  We went through some of the Factory Outlet stores but was not happy at what we found there.  We stopped at Hoggle Zoo and spent the afternoon going through it.  The weather was cloudy but very pleasant.  We next stopped at the Deseret Industries in Center Ville and found much better selection at less cost than at the outlet stores in Park City.  When we got home we watched a movie on DVD before going to bed.
