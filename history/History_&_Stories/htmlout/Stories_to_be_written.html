            Mom and Dad bought a home in Sunset the summer of 1955.  One day Dad went down to the little grocery store on the corner, he ask the owner if he has any “Cackle Berries”, to which the owner replied, “That at the time they were out of them.”  Dad then ask if he had any “Hens Fruit?”  To which the owner replied, “No, we will have to order some of those in.”  Dad then ask, “If he had any eggs, (of which both Cackle Berries and Hens Fruit are the same as eggs), to which the owned replied, “Oh yes, we have plenty of those.”
