            I didn't sleep very well last night so after breakfast went back to bed for a while.  I feel some better today so went to the World Wide Satellite Priesthood Leadership Broadcast.  The instructions were absolutely great but I felt very depressed as there were such a few Brothers there.  That is my biggest concern here is the Priesthood Leadership.  President Mercado, President Nuque, Brother Ernesto Damacio (I think) and I met for a brief District Presidency meeting.  They talk mainly in Tagalog so I really didn't feel very good about that meeting.  When I got home Mae had spoke to the lady next door, about how pretty the flowers were, but she must have misunderstood for she cut the flowers off.  This language is the hardest barrier I have ever tried to work around.  This evening we got some things ready to take to Guimba tomorrow.  I worked on my history writing more about the experiences I remember about my Father.

 

Sunday, June 29, 2003

 

            We were able to sleep in a little, then got up and went to the Guimba 2 Branch.  We attended their Sacrament Meeting.  They had a very nice meeting with the Jr. Sunday School giving the program.  I visited with the Branch Presidency about their Home Teaching program, and was very excited to see the progress they are making.  I gave them some helps.  They ask us to give a Home Teachers training class on the 12th of July, which we agreed to do.  Sister Ronda and Sister Cathleen visited with for a few minutes in between meetings.  We then met with the Guimba 1st Branch and gave them their Home Teaching records and some training and aids.  We attended the 1st Branches meetings.  It was a very enjoyable day.  We had Elder Jex's and Elder Barrett for dinner this evening and discussed the Home Teaching Fireside for next Saturday.  Mae worked on the Family letter until quite late.

 

Monday, June 30, 2003

 

            I woke early and wrote in my history.  We went grocery shopping, paid some bills, and got our money transferred at the bank.  After lunch, I got the car serviced; the tire repaired, filled the car with gas and got it washed.  Mae again stayed up late working on letters.  I fell asleep about 9PM.
