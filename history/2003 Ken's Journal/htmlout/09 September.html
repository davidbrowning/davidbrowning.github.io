            Requirements for a family to participate in this project are:

            1.         Must be residents of San Jose.

            2.         Must have a annual income of P10,000 or less.

            3.         Must be legally married.

            4.         Must have at least one child.

            5.         Must work 200 hours of volunteer work on the project within 2 months.

            6.         The family must repay some of the cost.

 

            Other questions that were answered for us are:

1.                  There are presently 40 duplex units, that are occupied in this project:

2.                  There are 55 units under construction now:
