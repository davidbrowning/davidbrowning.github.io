My Summery of BYU Education Week.

               

                This has been a great experience and in some ways almost over whelming.  We have felt hourly the Spirit of the Lord.  As I related to one Brother, that the closes synario that I could relate this experience to, was a story that I remember from when we lived in Ogden, Mount Fort Ward.  It was told of a dear Sister that came home from attending Church and as usual, there was the young man that, he and his new family lived in this Sister’s basement apartment.  He was out washing and polishing his car.  It seemed that he always had thing that took president over attending church meetings.  She walked over to the young man and tool him the she missed having he and his family attend Church.  He asked her why?  Then to make his point he continued.  I see you attend every week.  And she agreed, that she did.  Then he continued by asking, “What do you gain for your weekly attending meetings.  Do you remember what the announcements that was given?  To this she thought and said that the Bishop had given several but she just couldn’t recall what they were.  He then asked, “Who gave the opening prayer?  Again she remembered that the prayer was very sincere but she couldn’t remember who gave it.  He then ask what was the sermon was about, and who gave it?  To this she thought and reluctantly said that it was a very good sermon but she couldn’t recall the speakers name or the title of his talk.  See said the young man, you can’t recall any of the meeting, so what good does it do you to attend each week.  The Sister thought for a minute and then asked the young man if he would use the hose in his hand and fill the wicker basket there with water.  The young man laughed and replied that you couldn’t fill a wicker basket with water as it would run right out as fast as you put it in.  To this the Sister replied, I know, but the basket would be a lot cleaner.  This is the way I felt at the end of this week.  One speaker told us that if we would do the weekly and daily spiritual items that our leaders are asking us to do that we would be a closer to the Lord and would never fall away.
