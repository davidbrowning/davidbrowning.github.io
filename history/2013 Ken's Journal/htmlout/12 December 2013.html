                I couldn’t stay in bed longer, so got up shortly after 7AM and showered and shaved.  When I came downstairs, Connor was up playing video games.  I worked on my journal for a few minutes and then played a game of hearts, which I won for a change.  Soon the family was up and we had breakfast.  David and I visited for a while about the situation that the country is in.  I hope I didn’t upset him with my feelings.  I really drought if there is any that will change the trend that the country is headed for except for the coming of the Savior.  We played a couple of games after having breakfast.  Mae and I left about noon to go to Jeff and Gail’s.  We stopped on the way and got a Sub-Way for our lunch.  We enjoyed being with Jeff’s family this afternoon and evening.  We did a lot of visiting and playing games.  We spent the night here although Mae isn’t feeling the best again.

 

Friday, December 27, 2013

 

                After breakfast Jeff had some reports to run and he had extra time while the program ran the various sections of the reports.  To spend the extra time he and I played checkers on the computer.  Jeff won the game.  Jeff, Gail, Mae and I played a game of ‘Hand and Foot” and though it was close, Jeff and Gail won.  It was 3PM when we left for home.  After bringing things in from the car, I checked the chickens.  The two baby chicks seem to be doing fine.  I am afraid that the other eggs were not fertile.  I will give them a couple more days.  I did get 16 eggs so the chickens are doing better about laying eggs.  Mae is running a fever again this afternoon and evening.  This evening Mae and I watched a couple of shows on TV.  Bob assisted me in giving Mae a Priesthood Blessing this evening.

 

Saturday, December 28, 2013

 

                Mae is still sick so we took her into the Dr. and got a prescription.  It has been a very busy and stressful day for me.  We changed our plans and stayed home.

 

Sunday, December 29, 2013

 

                Mae didn’t sleep last night and is really miscible this morning.  I went to my High Priest Group leadership meeting but no one else was there so I came home.  I made arrangement for Shellie and Jase to stay at Jeff and Gail’s this evening.  Mae sure hasn’t felt well today.  I taught her Primary class for her today.  That went quite well.  Bob cooked some pork steaks this afternoon for us.  We just mainly relaxed this evening.

 

Monday, December 30, 2013

 

                Mae still isn’t feeling very well nor did she sleep well last night.  I tried to keep her down most of the day.  I had to have her tell me some of what I was to do with the weekly washing.  Shellie, Jase and Sandi went into see Nancy today and Shellie really came out very disappointed with her results.  Probably a lot of the problem was the long drive up and the tension that causes.  They stopped in for a few minutes after they went shopping for Mae’s Birthday.  We sure enjoyed their short visit with us but Sandi still had to drive all the way home.  It was late when we went to bed.

 

Tuesday, December 31, 2013

 

                Mae is still under the weather.  I got us some breakfast, went to the store for a few items and got some sawdust for the chicken nests.  I cleaned out the coop and took care of the chickens and the new chicks.  I got some bus seating forms ready so that on Thursday I can make seating assignments for the grade school students on my bus.   I watched a show on TV with Mae.  Just after 5PM Shellie came over and got me to go to Sandi’s.  it was so good to have a chance to visit with Shellie as we drove.  All of my family was there except for Jeff and his Family.  And of course Mae wasn’t there so that left a large vacancy in the Party.   Sandi and Nick had gone all out to make the gathering a great success.  I road home with Scott, and enjoyed being with him.  It has been a wonderful way to close out the year of 2013.  It always leaves the big question as what does 2014 have in store????
