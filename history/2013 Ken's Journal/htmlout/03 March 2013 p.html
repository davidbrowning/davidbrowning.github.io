                It was a pretty normal morning with Mae getting hot cakes and eggs for breakfast.  My runs went well and I stopped into the shop to schedule my bus to be serviced and to enter my field trip from yesterday.  Mae and I worked on the garden, the shop (Mae cleaned it up for me) and in the planter boxes.  I took my PM run and that went well.  I stopped at the store on the way home and got some items Mae needed.  David and Shauna brought their children about 5PM.  Mae made a chicken dinner and then David and Shauna left and we had the children stay over.  We played games until I had to go to bed.  Mae stayed up and continued to play games but I have to work in the morning so I went to bed.

 

Friday, March 29, 2013

 

                I wasn’t sure when Mae got to bed so I let her sleep in.  Connor woke while I was showering and was reading when I went into the front room.  My runs went well and when I returned Mae had waffles for breakfast.  I ate and took a nap.  Mae and the kids had gone bowling when I woke up.  I worked in the rose planters until time for the afternoon run.  Shauna had come and picked up the children by the time I got home.  Mae was laying down taking a nap.  She woke up when I got home.  We got things ready and went up to Jeff and Gail’s to tend the children while they went out.  We took the children for a walk around the block.  Mae took the children downstairs where they watched a movie on the TV.  I fell asleep on the couch in the front room.  When I woke up I went downstairs and watched the end of their movie.  By then Jeff and Gail were home so after they put the children down for the night we played a game before going home.  We soon got ready and went to bed.  Mae read a couple of articles out of the Ensign as we drove home.  I am having problems with becoming discouraged with some of the articles as I hear the writers tell how to raise families.  I thought that we had tried but I feel that we sure failed.

 

Saturday, March 30, 2013

 

                We got up about 7:30 and I started getting things set up outside for our family to join us about 1PM.  Mae prepared us some breakfast and then worked all morning getting the house and food ready.  When our family arrived between 1 and 1:30PM we let all the children hunt for Easter Eggs.  When Tyler, Emily and Connor had stayed over the other night Mae had them color Easter Eggs.  She also had made up plastic eggs in which she had candy and money.  I hid most of the eggs throughout the yards and Mae came out and helped me with the last of the eggs.  While the children we hunting Easter Eggs, they found a large rat that was dead.  We suspect that he was the one that killed one of our chicks, but he must have also eaten the Rat and Mouse poison I put out.   After the children found the eggs, most of the adults and older children played volleyball, and visited, While Mae & Mishelle took the younger children over to the park to play.  About 5PM we barbecued chicken, hamburger and hot dogs for dinner along with all the other trimmings that was brought by each family.  We had all our sons and their Families with us except for David, our grandson.  Page came and brought her boy friend Garrett.  It looks like they are getting quite serious.  It was a wonderful day but by the time they all left Mae and I were very tired.  I went with Brother Thomas home teaching to Sister Olsen’s. 

 

Sunday, March 31, 2013

 

                We slept in this morning.  It was Fast Sunday so we didn’t eat this morning.  In our High Priest Group meeting I gave the lesson.  Mae also helped with the lesson in the Relief Society meeting.  Bob had family come to visit this morning and then this afternoon he went to Grant’s for dinner with his family.  This evening we watched the movie “The Ten Commandments” upstairs with Bob.  He had recorded it from the other night.  That helped as we could fast forward the film through the advertisement.  It was still after mid-night when we finished the show and came downstairs.
