Wednesday, February 01, 2012

 

                Mae didn’t sleep very well last night and ended up sleeping most of the night in the recliner.  When I got up I tried not to wake her.  After showering and getting dressed Mae woke.  I tried to get her to go back to bed but she insisted on getting me some breakfast.  My route went well and after completing it I went in and visited with Janet Nance about two days that I drove bus that was not put on the payroll.  When I got home Mae had gone shopping.  As soon as I walked in Bob knocked.  There is many concerns about the funeral arrangements for Eric’s Fathers funeral.  Bob got a hold of Sherri and had her call me.  I will see what I can do to help.  I took care of the animals and then came in and then made some phone calls.  I had Sherrie have Eric’s Uncle call me.  He did while I was o my bus run but we were at a stop where I could talk to him.  I found out that everything was under control and ready for Eric’s father’s funeral.  Sherrie had miss-understood several things.  The afternoon run went well and I was home about 4:30PM.  Mae and I had planned on going to the Temple but I felt that I should go down to Sherri’s and explain some things to them.  We tried a couple of times to call them but they didn’t return the calls.  I worked on my journals most of the evening and Mae worked on the other computer.  Later I went upstairs and visited with Bob.  I told him that I had tried to call Sherrie but that she did not return my call.  He called and she answered right off.  He tried to explain some of the things that I wanted to explain to her but he didn’t understand what I tried to tell him or he just didn’t understand what I meant.  Anyway I felt bad that I couldn’t get my feelings across to her.  I am afraid that I very often come across to other in a wrong way. 

 

Thursday, February 02, 2012

 

                My morning run went well.  Some of these handicap children really humble you.  We pickup one little boy, his mother always brings him out to the bus.  She is tall, dark hair fairly good looking, very pleasant and always has a smile.  This morning when they came out the little boy was fighting her.  He didn’t want to come.  He didn’t seem to want to leave his dog and I don’t think the dog wanted to leave him for as they came out the dog came also and the mother had to chase him back while trying to get her son out.  She literally had to pick the boy up in both arms and carry him kicking to the bus.  As she greeted us at the bus she said through an almost forced smile, “it has been one of those mornings”.  She was smiling and waved as I pulled the bus away.  For the next mile I really had to work to keep control of my empathy for this young mother.  Even as I type this the tears are flowing.  Bob prepared fish and fried potatoes for lunch and it was very good.  I took a short nap before my afternoon run.  After getting home Mae told me some of the conversation she had with Michelle.  I have been very depressed the rest of the night.  Mae and I attended a session at the Bountiful Temple.  I was on the verge of tears through most of the session. 

 

 Friday, February 03, 2012

 

                I again took the bus run.  After returning home I fed the chickens and rabbits and then took a short nap.  Mae and I went over to the funeral of Erric’s father, Larin J. Peterson.  I felt sad that none of Sherrie’s siblings were there to support them.  I had to leave early as I had the afternoon run to take.  Mae stayed.  I completed the run and when I got home I entered my payroll time and bid on upcoming field trips.  Mae and I went to the T-Mobil phone store trying to get her a phone.  She has tried ordering one through the phone but that hasn’t worked.  At the store they told us that we would have to wait until the phone order expired and then come back.  I have questions that the order over the phone might have been too good of a deal and so the reprehensive let it fall through.  I guess we will just have to wait and see.  We then went to Wall Mark and I got my new eye glasses.  I then took Mae out to dinner at Golden Corral.  After dinner we went over to Scott’s and visited with Scott and the children as Michelle had gone with her mother and sisters.  I went to bed very tired and somewhat depressed.

 

Saturday, February 04, 2012

 

                We woke early to attend a Family History convention in Salt Lake.  Mae read from the Ensign as I drove and I became very depressed.  I almost became sick before we got there.  My depression is coming from my three boys and I do not know what to do about it.  We did attend the program and it was amazing but I felt so over whelmed and personally very inadequate for my calling.  When we got home we laid down to take a nap.  We had only got to sleep when Bob knocked to see if we were home.  I couldn’t go to sleep after that.  We took dinner up to Jeff and Gail’s this evening.  After eating I took a nap.  Mae played with the grandchildren.  After the children were put to sleep Mae, Gail, Jeff and I played games.  We finished with a game of Rook at which Jeff and I won.  It was late when we got home.

 

Sunday, February 05, 2012

 

                We were able to sleep in and sure enjoyed that.  In our group leadership meeting this morning Brother Thomas told me that I was to give the lesson.  I had a feeling that I was going to do that when I left home, so I brought both Ensign’s with me.  I had a few minutes to go back through the lesson as we had already read the article.  I asked for help in a short prayer and I seem to have done ok with some appreciations expressed after.  Following Sacrament meeting Mae and I got interviews for new Temple Recommends.  Bob soon had dinner ready when we got home.  After dinner Mae and I laid down for a nap.  We hadn’t slept long when Bob came down with some questions about Family Research.  Trying to get on Family Search to help Bob plus other phone calls soon took the rest of the day.  I got a call to take a bus run in the morning and so had to get to bed.  Mae stayed up talking to Georgia on the phone.

 

Monday, February 06, 2012

 

                I got up early and was to the bus shop in Farmington early.  I went to the first pickup getting there early but when I approached the stop right on time, a boy asked me if this was the bus for Wood Cross High.  I said that it was so he got on.  There was no other students at the stop and yet the rout sheet indicated that there should be 18.  I asked the student where they were and he said that that was not a normal stop.  I asked where the next one was and he showed me where to turn.  When I asked him where from there he didn’t know.  I called the dispatch and by then I was lost.  Even with dispatch’s help I was still lost and when I got to the third stop there was only 3 students.  When I got to the high school Dispatch said that they had another bus get my Jr. High students, so for me to get ready to get my grade school.  I replied that according to my route sheets I didn’t have a grade school route.  They faxed me a route sheet to the office of Wood Cross.  With that route sheet I was able to complete that pickup with out to much trouble.  After returning the bus to the compound I drove to the Clearfield compound and went into dispatch to discuss the route sheets.  We found out that the sheets were very wrong, plus I visited with Roberta and got ather routes to take for the rest of the week that does not conflict with my field trips.  It was 10:30 when I left to go home.  I took a nap and then we had lunch.  I took a special ed. Run this afternoon and it went well.  I stopped in the office, when I finished the route and Rose call to me and asked if I would take a run tomorrow, which I agreed to do.  Mae had pizza for dinner this evening.  I had Bob come down and I showed him that his Family History was in order for his children to continue with any research they would like to do.  Mae went up to Jeff and Gail’s to tend their children this evening.  Bob and I visited for a while about Dad Browning and some of his stories.  I worked on my journal until after 9PM and so was late getting to bed.  I woke at 11PM realizing that Mae was not in bed.  I got up to check on her and found that she had just returned and was ready to come to bed. 

 

Tuesday, February 07, 2012

 

                I woke again at 3AM but tried to go back to sleep.  After a while I got up and did some studding.  I ate breakfast and left for the bus trip.  My runs went well and I made all the schedules on time.  When I got back to the shop the temperatures had risen above 32 degrees and the bus wash was open for use.  I took the bus through it as it needed it quite badly.  It was after 10 AM when I got home and Mae had already left to take Shirley Olsen to a doctor’s appointment.  I got something to eat and then took a long nap.  Mae got home just before noon.  We had lunch together and had just finished when Gail brought the children for Mae to tend while she went to the Temple.  I blow up balloons for the children to play with.  I got this entry started before I left for the afternoon runs.  My run went well.  This evening Bob and I went Home Teaching to Casey’s and Johnston’s.  With the Casey’s, as normal we were only a few minutes.  When we went to the Johnston’s we were there almost 2 hours.  Some of that time was checking Sister Johnston’s printer and found that it was completely out of ink.  I told her that if they could buy some new cartridges I would install them for her.  We soon went to bed after I got home. 

 

Wednesday, February 08, 2012

 

                This morning I had a Field Trip to take and that went well.  when I got back to the compound, Roberta called me to tell me that the Field Trip I had scheduled for this afternoon was canceled.  She did have another route that needed covered this afternoon, so I went home and got some dinner and a nap.  I then took the afternoon route.  Mae and I watched “Little House On The Prairie” on TV while we ate our dinner.  At 7PM we went to the Stake House to get our Temple Recommends signed.  Brother Thomas and went visiting Brother Therman and Brother Martin.  When I got home I filled out this entry.  It was after 9PM and we are never sure if I will get a call early in the morning so we soon went to bed.  I did get a call about 10PM asking me to take a route tomorrow.

 

Thursday, February 09, 2012

Friday, February 10, 2012

Monday, February 13, 2012

I had told Roberta yesterday that I could not take a bus route this morning because of Vica Germer, Jean Knutson’s mother, funeral today.  Also I have a field trip this afternoon.  However I did get a call at 5:25AM to see if I could take a morning run.  I told them I could not because of the funeral.  I prepared breakfast and after eating I took care of the animals and then went to the church and help set up tables and chairs for the Family Lunching.  We had five of us there so it went quite fast.  Mae and I attended the viewing and funeral for Vica Hall Germer.  We had the privilege of knowing Vica for the short time she lived here in our Ward with Brother and Sister Knudsen.  Vica was the Mother to Sister Jean Knudsen.  We thought a lot of her.  Her Nephew, that gave the closing prayer, said just before he gave the prayer, that when you stopped to see her for a minute that they called it “the Vica grip” for she loved to keep you and visit.  It was a very special and appropriate funeral.  I got a nap in and then took a field trip.  That went very well and I got home about 8:30PM.  Mae went back to the church and helped serve the Family Dinner for the funeral.  She then went to Deanna’s for a treatment.  I entered my payroll entries and finished my journal entry before we went to bed.
