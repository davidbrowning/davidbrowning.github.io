Sunday, April 01, 2012

 

                I woke early this morning with still a realization of my weaknesses in the way I speak to my sweetheart.  I realize that I am very weak in many of my characteristic and know that I need help to be the person worthy of my companion and the companionship on my Lord and Savior.  The weather has changed and is now storming.  I put my boat back away and took care of the chickens and rabbits.  I am afraid that my one white doe is not well and wonder if I should butcher her now or wait to see if she gets better.  Mae prepared French toast for breakfast.  After eating we enjoyed the Morning Session of General Conference.  I called Randy and we decided to change our fishing trip until next Thursday, due to the bad weather.  I also called Tyler and we will try to take him, Emily and Connor fishing on Friday.   Mae made a nice lunch and we had Bob come down to eat with us.  This afternoon we again enjoyed the last Session of General Conference.  Mae and I drove over to the rehabilitation center to visit Sister Joanne Von Niederhauser.  She is doing very well.  We didn’t stay long as they had family there.  Mae worked on pictures of her mother’s that I had digitized before we left on our first mission.  She used the desk top computer this evening while I used the lap top to work on my journals.  It takes me quite a while as I am digitizing my Five Year Diary from 1970 to 1974.  This is when I started keeping a diary.  We went into our bedroom and watched another J.A.G. series before we went to sleep.

 

Monday, April 02, 2012

 

                We slept in this morning as our school district is on Spring Break for this week.  After breakfast I fed the animals and then dug post holes and set standards for a volley ball net and two fence post.  I mixed cement and set the post and a base for the standards.  Bob invited us up for lunch as he had made a very good stew.  This afternoon Mae and I went up to Cindy Bennet’s and got three more rabbits from her.  The one has an inner ear infection and has lost its equilibrant.  I just put it out of its suffering.  The other two are ok to rise for meat.  We stopped on the way home and bought chicken and rabbit feed.  We then changed vehicles and went into Ogden where I bought a set of scales.  We stopped at Good Earth and sold them 5 dozen eggs and bought some other things we needed.  On the way home from there we bought a Sub-Way sandwich for dinner.  Mae went up to Jeff and Gail’s where she tended their children while Gail went to a class.  Jeff is out of town on business.  I worked on Indexing for over 2 ½ hours getting one batch done this evening.  I watched the last of one show but Mae still hadn’t got home and it was after 10pm.  I started another show when Mae got home so we watched it.  It was after mid-night before we got to bed.

 

Tuesday, April 03, 2012

 

                We slept in but I went pretty hard the rest of the day.  I took care of the animals, went over to Scott’s and put a 2nd coat of mud on the basement.  Mae had lunch ready when I got home.  I did take a short nap and then trimmed the pear tree and the plum tree.  I removed the one plum tree and hauled it away.  Bob and I then moved the fence on the east side of the garden and got it almost finished before coming in for super.  After eating I entered the entry and we soon went to bed.  Mae has been busy with family Easter plans; Siblings get to gather and the Bushnell family reunion.  She also went shopping and bought a volley ball and net.

 

Wednesday, April 04, 2012

 

                I woke up at 4am and found 2 notes from Mae.  She hadn’t been to sleep by 1:30am and the 2nd was that she hadn’t been to sleep until after 3am.  I let her sleep and got myself off.  Randy and I went fishing at East Canon reservoir.  We got on the lake just as the sun rose.  We had a good time and had good luck.  We caught the last of our limits about 1pm and was home about 3pm.  Mae had been with Michelle and the children to Dinosaur Park and had just got home before I did.  Scott came to our place when he got off work.  Mae prepared dinner for all of us.  Scott and I started to set up the volley ball net but found out that the measurements we used did not fit the net Mae bought.  I will have to set a new hole for the one pole.  After Scott, Michelle and the children left we watched a couple of western movies on TV before we went to bed.

 

Thursday, April 05, 2012

 

                We slept in somewhat, as Bob prepared breakfast of biscuits and gravy and baked fish.  We ate at 9am.  I had fed the animals before we ate.  I set the garden up to water with the hose.  The secondary water isn’t on yet and the ground is very dry.  I removed the west volleyball standard and reset it further west.  I took Bob with me and we purchased salt for the water softener, bark to put around the rose bushes, cement to reset the volleyball standard and parts to remodel the garden gate.  Mae boiled 6 dozen eggs this morning and then Gail and their children came over and enjoyed coloring them.  I got all the jobs done before we went to the Bountiful Temple this evening.  I am experiencing quite a bit of pain this evening.  After getting home from the Temple I made calls on last months Home Teaching reports.

 

Friday, April 06, 2012

 

                As I entered the date I couldn’t help but realize that it has been 182 years ago today that the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints was officially organized.  It is also the anniversary day of the birth of Our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.  It has snowed quite heavy during the night and is still snowing lightly at 10am.  I have talked to David and we determined that we would cancel plans to take Tyler and Connor fishing today.  I have taken care of my animals and now going to try indexing another batch of 1940 census.  I worked on today’s batch for over 4 hours.  It was a difficult patch because of the poor handwriting of the one taking the census.  Mae and I laid in the recliners and took a short nap and then went into Ogden.  We stopped at AmeriGas to see if they can repair the gas tank for the camper but they said that the company that makes that tank doesn’t make parts, they just sell new tanks.  We left and went to Cosco for a few items.  After that we went out to eat at Chuck-A-Rama.  This evening we watched a show on TV before going to bed.

 

Saturday, April 07, 2012

 

                The weather was perfect for Mae’s Easter Party with our family.  All the boys and their families came.  I set up the volleyball standards and net and that was a good success with a lot of fun.  The children had fun finding Easter Eggs.  Everyone helped with dinner items and so lunch went well.  The boys helped with the putting away tables and the volley ball net and standards.  The children had fun gathering the eggs from the chickens.  Ben found that some of the chickens are laying eggs underneath the storage shed so I nailed boards to stop them from getting underneath the shed again.  It was about 7pm when they all went home.  I helped Mae finish putting the toys away.  Bob came down just as I turned on a show on TV.  He stayed and watched it with me.  After the show I helped Mae vacuum the apartment.  We got things ready to go to Powder Ridge tomorrow evening.  It is almost 10pm and we still need to take showers.  So we will go to bed, very tired.

 

Sunday, April 08, 2012

 

                I woke at 3:45am and didn’t sleep well after that.  When Mae got up she said that she didn’t sleep very well either.  We must have been too tired after yesterday’s activity.  It is a beautiful Easter morning.  I took care of the animals and while doing that I saw Brother Kelley Bodily out so I called to him with a good morning.  He said it was until he walked down stairs and found that his water heater was leaking.  I loaned him my wet vacuum and he said that was enough.  We attended our normal Sunday meetings.  Mae and I stayed for a short choir practice.  Bob had dinner ready when we got home.  Bob’s family came over just as we were finishing dinner.  His grand children wanted to see the chickens and rabbits so I went out with them so that they wouldn’t chase them like they did last time.  They had upset the chickens until they quite laying for a couple of days.  I felt bad that I couldn’t be in helping Mae get things ready to go.  We did get away about 5PM, stopping on the way at Kim’s home to pick up some items they had gone off without.  When we got to Powder Ridge and located the condo, Bryce and Deanna and Kim and Jeanenne were already there.  Rick and Georgia soon came.  We enjoyed a good discussion lead by Rick about the Crucifixion of Our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.  We also watched an excellent movie of John Tanner.  It has been a very interesting and an enjoyable evening.  It was after mid-night before we got to bed.

Tuesday, April 10, 2012

 

                I slept much better as Mae changed beds with me.  She sleeps on her back where I sleep on my sides so the softer bed helped me and Mae slept ok on the firmer bed.  We got up about 7:30am and started the day.  I wasn’t able to read my scriptures because my IPod was low on power and I had forgotten to bring the connecting cable to my lap top.  We packed and cleaned up the condo and had a very nice breakfast with everyone helping.  We got everything ready to leave and then took time to watch the last of the movie we started last night.  The title was “Second Hand Lion” and it was sure a cute movie.  After getting home I took a short nap before we ate lunch.  Mae then went over to Scott and Michelle’s where she tended Kaitlin while Michelle took Kaden to the Dr. for a checkup.  He is feeling much better.  I went to work on the pickup and installed new front break on it.  The old ones were completely gone and were just starting to scratch the brake drums.  I got a couple of calls on my bus route this afternoon.  First they informed me that I am getting another student to take home after school.  The sub-driver called me and said that my bus was in the shop for repair and I would have to get a sub bus for tomorrow morning.  This evening Mae and Ashley attended a special Grandmother and Grand Daughters Relief Society program.  I sure appreciated Bob’s help while I was repairing the pickup.  I came in and got myself some super at PM.  I turned on the TV but the show “Air Force 1” was rated R so I turned to another channel and saw that “The Other Side of Heaven” was just starting so I watched that.  Mae got home before it finished.  She said that they had a very good evening.  We watched the end of the show and then I entered my journal entry for today.  Good-night!
