Sunday, April 05, 2015

To my family

 

                With the only exception, and that is your Mother, you are the dearest and greatest people I have in life.  My heart is full of love for each of you.  I don’t know if I have the ability to express my feelings of love, concerns and desires that I have for each of you.  I tremble with concern as to whether I can relay in words these feelings in such a way that none of you will take offence at what I desire to express to each member of my family from my oldest daughter, Sandra Lee to my youngest  great-grandson Luke.

                I remember that as a teenager, a Sunday school teacher, had each in his class write our GOALS we wanted to achieve in this life.  I am not sure if I had ever done this before but as I thought about my goals to accomplish, I wrote three Main goals I wanted to accomplish.  1st I wanted to serve a mission.  2nd I wanted to marry a beautiful girl in the Temple.  3rd I wanted to raise a family that would be honorable people and righteous servants to our Lord.
