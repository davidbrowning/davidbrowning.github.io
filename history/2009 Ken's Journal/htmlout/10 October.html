                I got up at 7:30 AM but my palm said that it was Day Light Saving time.  I turned our computer on to check for sure and it didn’t indicate any change in time.  Oh well!  I guess I have had enough sleep.  I prepared breakfast while Mae put up her hair.  I fed the animals before getting ready for meetings.  Chelci sang “How Great Thou Art” in our Sacrament meeting with Sandi accompanying her.  The arrangement was not the standard arrangement that is in the Hymn book but much more complicated.  Sandi has been practicing it for several weeks and was very nervous, but she did beautiful.  I told them after that there was one problem with it and that was that I had popped all the buttons off my shirt.  I love that song and wish that I could have them play it at my funeral.  After the meeting they stopped at home long enough to take some apples, potatoes, grapes and a small squash.  They had to hurry home as Chelci had to do more studying for school.  Bob invited us for dinner and it was delicious.  I did take a short nap and then Bob and I went Home Teaching to Patriarch Johnstun’s and Sister Fallis’s.  it took us a lot longer than I wanted but both were difficult to close.  Mae had eaten by the time I got home so I had some fruit and toast and then filled in this entry.  Mae is busy preparing for a talk she has to give I a couple of weeks.  I had better get to bed as I have to get up about 4:30AM to keep and appointment with Chelci to clean my teeth in the morning.

 

Monday, October 26, 2009

 

                I got up early but let Mae sleep in.  I got to the Salt Lake Community Collage about 7:30AM so I called Mae and had her return my call about 7:55AM.  That way I could sleep for 20 minutes before going in to have Chelci work on my teeth.  Chelci really work on my teeth and the most time was in getting the x-rays.  It was 11:30 AM before she finished and I will need to come back for her to finish up.  When I got home we had some lunch and I lay down and took a nap.  I didn’t get much done this afternoon except to take care of the chickens and clean the coop.  Mae and I tried to set the timer but were unsuccessful and finally gave up.  Our printer quite working and after checking on prices it looks like we will just have to buy a new one.  This evening we went over to Scott and Mishelle’s for dinner and to celebrate Kaden and Kaitlyn’s 1st Birthday anniversary.  Mishelle’s parents were also there.  Mishelle and Scott had prepared a very nice dinner.  Before leaving I went downstairs and checked a leak in their water softener and found that the case on the timer was cracked.  I will have to check on a replacement.  It was late when we got home and we soon went to bed.

 

Tuesday, October 27, 2009

 

                We got up with plans to go to Nancy this morning.  I took care of the animals and we had breakfast.  I called to check on the water softener for Scott and we estimate that his is over 30 years old so he will need a new unit.  We were just ready to leave when Nancy called and said that her computer was having problems so she was taking it to Provo for repairs and would not be able to keep her appointments today.  I got on our computer and had some problems getting the internet to respond so I finally called for help and was on line with a technician for over an hour.  He told me that I needed to hook the router to the computer and that would take me a little while so he would call me back.  I did get the router changed to the desktop but the technician never did call me back.  Scott came for lunch and while he was here he checked the computer and the wireless router is now working.  This afternoon I took a nap and then I went on errorns.  I got natural gas and regular gas in the pickup; I stopped at Young’s Chevrolet and got a scamatic of the dash of the pickup.  I also checked on the price of the switch and the cost of labor to install it.  The switch cost $237.00 and $169.00 to have it installed, WHO!  I next stopped at Home Depot and got some help on the timer switch that I had installed for the lights in the chicken coop.  I will try again to set it.  I took a pair of cowboy boots to Ray’s shoe repair in Kaysville, then went to Best Buy in Riverdale and bought a new printer as ours quite on us yesterday.  I stopped at Aaron’s Jewelry in Roy and picked up my new watch.  Mae had dinner ready when I got home.  We ate and went to the Family History Center.  After returning home I entered this journal entry and we soon went to bed.

 

Wednesday, October 28, 2009

 

                I got up before 7AM to get things done before going to a Sub-Bus Drivers Round Table.  That lasted from 9:30 to 11:15AM.  I then stayed to take care of some other items.  I stopped on the way home and bought some feed for the animals.  After getting home I took the feed out to the shed and unloaded it.  I got something to eat and while I ate I watched the last of a movie on TV.  I tried to take a nap but Bob called shortly after I got to sleep.  I decided to get the new printer hooked up.  Six hours later and with 2 hours of a Qwest Technician and then 2 hours of help from Scott, we finally got it to print in black and white only.  The colored still doesn’t work.  I made this entry before going to bed.  Mae has spent most of the day helping Mishelle with the children and letting her get some rest.  Mae is sending a E-mail off to Erryn Tam’s for me concerning sealing ordinances for Mom and Leo.  We then read a letter for Shellie before we went to bed. 

 

Thursday, October 29, 2009

 

                Mae and I didn’t sleep to well last night.  I was concerned about the water softener for Scott’s home.  I don’t feel very good at trying to get it to do until after Christmas.  Mae went over to help Mishelle with the children and to attend a Halloween Parade at Kylan and Ashley’s school.  I took care of the animals and tried to reset the light program but still couldn’t get it set.  I called Best Buy and told them about the trouble I am having with the printer that I just bought and they want me to return it and if they can’t find the trouble they will give me another one.  Now it is just finding time to take it back.  Mae came back for Mishelle’s just in time to go help Sister Marie McEntire with the New Family Search program.  I tried to take a nap and did get to sleep for a few minutes when Sandy called and had a question about the possibility of Kristine remarrying and possibility even marring in the Temple.  I told her my thoughts on it and she felt ok with that.  Mae got home in time to fix me some lunch before I left to take a field trip.  Mae is going to attend the Temple tonight as it is our Stake’s Temple Night before Stake Conference.  I would have liked to attended with her.  I took the Swim Team from North Ridge High to Bear River High in Garland.  I was assigned bus #348 and it was really a nice bus.  It was a good experience with the youth and the bus.  I didn’t get back to the compound and finished with the bus until 9:35PM.  Mae had just got home.  I entered my journal and we went to bed.

 

Friday, October 29, 2009

 

                I had recertification training today at the bus compound.  It is a State Law that all bus drivers take this course yearly or they will not be able to continue to drive.  It was ok but a lot was redundant information but I guess necessary.  It does upset me that we are given certain things that must be done, yet there are many drivers that do not do them.  Mea had put clothing and food together for us to go out to Sandi’s.  When I finished the class and got home we got things put into the truck and left.  We took the pickup because I also took some tools and ladders so that I could hang some chandeliers for Sandi.  This evening Sandi had invited all the family to attend a Halloween Party.  Everyone was asked to wear costumes to the party.  Sandi had rented the club house at Tooele Army Depot to hold the party.  Sandi had Chelci and JaNae help and it was really a nice evening.  After the party Mae and I went back to Sandi and Nicks where we stayed over night.  Before going to bed we had a game of marbles and Nick and I won.

 

Saturday, October 31, 2009

 

                We slept in and when we got up Nick had prepared breakfast of scrambled eggs and sautéed vegetables with mushrooms.  After breakfast I started hanging the chandeliers.  Sandi and Mae helped me unpackaged and assemble the chandeliers.  Nick went to the store and purchased light bulbs for the chandeliers and then when we had to use my saw horses to assemble some stair scaffolding and put my ladder on it to get me high enough to hang the chandelier, Nick insisted on holding to the ladder so that there was no chance that the ladder would slip.  It all went well and we completed the job.  Sandi and Nick thanked me for doing it several times before we left for home.  After getting home I went out and fed the animals.  Bob had fed them this morning.  Mae helped me and I think we were able to get the lighting timer set correctly.  This evening Mae and I watched a couple of movies, one on TV and the other on CD.
