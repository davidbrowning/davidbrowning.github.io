                We started getting up about 7AM.  The restroom that we parked close to was locked and we had to go to the restroom by the dam parking lot.  Again I couldn’t help thinking of the pioneers and as cold as the restroom was it was a lot better than they had it.  The day was just spent just being together.  Games were played, food was available, and we watched a cute cartoon movie that was very moving, entitled “Up”.  Some of us got a nap and some went to the Dam Store for a few items that we had forgotten to bring.  We went out fishing again about 9PM.  Fishing was slower tonight, although David did very well.  I think that we came out to early but before min-night I was cold and my back hurt so we came back.  It was so warm in the camper that we lost courage to go back out again. 

 

Saturday, November 28, 2009

 

                Again we started getting up about 7AM and soon had breakfast and started packing up.  With all of us working together we were on our way home by 10:15AM.  We stopped in I.F. where I bought some tie down straps that I fastened the cover onto my fishing tent slay.  On Jeff I-Phone he and I played a game of chess.  I was able to win him.  Later I challenged David (my grandson) to a game and I was able to beat him also.  Jeff was telling me of a family in Mexico that he met on his mission that refused to buy their family a TV, but instead, bought them books and a chess board.  He said that all the family became excellent chess players as well as very good readers.  David let Jeff and I off at Bryce and Deanna’s as she had left my pickup there.  We transferred our things from David’s truck and trailer to my pickup.  I then took Jeff home before going on home myself.  I got home between 4 and 4:30PM.  We were going to go to a basket ball game that Tyler was to play in but before we left David called and said that it had been canceled.  That is a shame because that is why we came home today.  I finished cleaning and filleting the fish we caught last night.  Mae and I then spent some time working on our talks for tomorrow, before showering and going to bed.

 

Sunday, November 29, 2009

 

                We got up and I took care of the animals.  We didn’t eat breakfast as both of us felt that we wanted to fast today before giving out talks.  We attended meetings and they were all very good.  With it being the fifth Sunday the Relief Society and the Melchizedek Priesthood met together and Bishop Smith presented the lesson.  He used Elder Holland’s talk from General Conference.  Bishop played the talk in it’s fullness after he added his testimony of the message.  Sunday school went well with Brother Thompson giving the lesson.  Mae and I spoke in Sacrament meeting.  Sister Page sang “Window’s of Love”, accompanied by Sister Saunders and it was very beautifully done.  That started a change in my talk and I ended up changing 90% of my presentation.  Scott, Mishelle, Kylan, Ashley, Kaden and Kaitlyn came to support us and Mae invited them to come over for dinner.  Mae prepared a nice dinner and I cooked the chicken on the grill.  We enjoyed having them with us.  Irven and Sandy came over and we played games and visited until late.  I took the garbage out to the curb, locked the chickens up for the night and put the grill away.  I then entered this journal entry before we went to bed.  It’s been a good day.  I did work in a few minutes to enter some of my school bus times on the computer.
