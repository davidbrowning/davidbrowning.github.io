TESTIMONY of S. Mark Hulet

Easter Sunday 23 March 2008

 

I have a testimony born of the Spirit, that our Father in Heaven lives, and that His son Jesus Christ lives at His right hand, both are resurrected glorified beings, assisted by the Holy Ghost, a person of spirit.  We refer to them as the Godhead.

 

I testify Our Father's Great Plan of Happiness was taught in pre-mortality to His spirit children, of which I was one.  I was privileged to be born when the gospel of Jesus Christ was on this earth in it's fullness, born in this Promised Land of freedom, and blessed with a family of goodly parents and siblings, all who honor their covenants with the Lord. 

 

I testify our Father in Heaven's Great Plan of Happiness is designed to save all children He created, and gives each one opportunity to accept or reject it, either in this life or in the post mortal world to come.

 

With faith I testify that at the center of Father's Plan is Jesus Christ, His first born spirit offspring, who volunteered before the foundation of this world to be the sacrificial lamb for the miraculous infinite atonement.  He atoned for the sins of all our Father's children on the many worlds He created and made Our Father's Plan successful.  When the Father said "Whom shall I send?"  Jesus answered "Here am I, send me."  Because of the great love Father in Heaven has for each of his children, He permitted Jesus Christ, who was to be His only begotten Son in the flesh, a Pre-mortal God, the Great Jehovah, sinless and perfect, to condescend (descend) below all things on earth, below all evil, to experience the consequence, the punishment, of the combined sins of all people God has created in all worlds He has made.  Hence, the atonement of Jesus Christ is infinite.

 

Our Father in Heaven allowed His Son, Jesus Christ, to suffer alone in the Garden of Gethsemane, except sending an angel, believed to be Gabriel, to comfort Him. In D&C Sec.19 Jesus describes his intense suffering in His own words; "Which suffering caused myself, even God, the greatest of all, to tremble because of pain, and bleed at every pore, and to suffer both body and spirit---and would that I might not drink the bitter cup and shrink."  In this Garden He cried out "O my Father, if it be possible, let this cup pass from me, Nevertheless, not as I will, but as thou wilt." His sacrifice was completed by crucifixion, a most cruel, vicious, and painful punishment, nailed to a cross, in public humiliation by the most wicked and conspiring men in the most wicked world of the millions God has created.  (From Mediation and Atonement by President John Taylor) Jesus Christ came to earth to do the will of the Father and His most significant and final words from the cross were "Father, it is finished, thy will is done." (Inspired Translation of the Bible) 

 

I can testify in full faith and sincerity that Jesus Christ lived on earth, died for our sins to redeem us from the Fall of Adam and from our own fall as we have all sinned and broken the laws of God.  He died for the purpose of bringing our sin account to zero, therefore, to make us just, and by shedding His blood cleansed, purified, and sanctified us to be worthy to live in the Celestial Kingdom with God and our families.  All prophets, beginning with Adam and to the present, have born this testimony of Jesus Christ; many sealed it with a martyr's blood.  The testimonies of these noble men, including our living prophet, are evidence for my faith and testimony.  As I listen, read, and study, the Spirit testifies to me the truth of their testimonies.

 

I know through the confirming influence of the Holy Ghost, by feelings and sensations, that He has been a near companion much of my life.  I believe He knows my heart seeks to be right with God, in spite of my weaknesses and mistakes, and by repentance, the principle of progress, He knows I strive to do better.

 

I have recorded many personal and sacred accounts of the Spirit of the Lord's communications with me; healing me at age 4 years, after my father's blessing promised I would live when the doctor said I would be dead in the morning; protecting me from death or serious injury in severe accidents; the spirit speaking in a firm audible voice which shook me, saying "Don't" when I made an unwise decision that would have altered my whole life. 

 

Many other sacred recorded personal experiences convince me God is real, He lives, He is the father of my spirit.  I have a testimony Jesus Christ, is the Son of God, also a God, came to earth to redeem me from physical death and spiritual death by His voluntary sacrifice for my sins, as he did for all others.  I know that Jesus Christ was resurrected from the dead, a miraculous event we honor and celebrate today, and opened the way for me and all Father's children to receive a perfect resurrected body in the form of our spirit.  We are all first generation spirit offspring of a perfect Father and Mother in Heaven.  The Church of Jesus Christ of former days and of Latter Day Saints is the one and only true Church of Jesus Christ ever to exist on this earth and in eternity, and President Thomas S. Monson is God's prophet for His church at this time.  I so testify, in the name of Jesus Christ, Amen.
