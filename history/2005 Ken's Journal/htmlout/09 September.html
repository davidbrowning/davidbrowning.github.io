Thursday, September 1, 2005

 

                I got up by 5 AM so I could read my scriptures and be ready to go to work early.  Bob prepared biscuits and gravy for breakfast this morning.  Bob, Bruce and I started removing shingles by 7 AM.  Bob was soon very tired so I had him quite and get down.  When we got the one side cleaned off, I started laying new shingles while Bruce kept removing the old shingles from the other side.  I took a short nap after we had lunch.  About 5 PM we quite again and I took a short nap.  We worked a little longer until Micheline had dinner ready.  After eating I again laid shingles until dark.  I went downstairs at 9:30 PM and wrote my journal entry before showering.  When I got out of the shower I found that Mae had sent me her garments by mistake. 

 

Friday, September 2, 2005

 

                We didn’t get up until 5 AM again this morning.  Both Bob and I slept in recliners last night as we didn’t sleep to good the night before.  My stomach was bothering me when we went to bed last night.  Bob tried to help us again today but he only lasted for a few minutes.  I had him get down as I was worried about his health.  I finished shingling the back side as Bruce worked on stripping the front.  We had to strip the front right to the sheathing as that side was completely deteriorated.  I again took a nap after lunch.  We worked until almost 5 PM when we got down and I took another short nap.  We worked a while longer and then ate dinner and worked until dark.  It really helped me when Bruce was able to keep me supplied with shingles.  After stopping for the day, Bruce, Micheline and I visited for a while and ate watermelon.  Mae called and we visited for a few minutes.  She had attended her aunt Merlynn’s funeral today.  Mark had came up to get Bob and Mae sent me up some garments with him.  Mark and Bob went up to Victor to attend a school reunion.  I was very tired tonight.

 

Saturday, September 3, 2005

 

                We got an early start on the roof but soon found that we were out of shingles.  I called Mae and had her go to Roofer Supply in Ogden and get me another square of shingles and a package of Ridge and Hip shingles.  I had thought that 2 packages of shingles would do it, but as I made the order I asked for a full square.  I was sure glad I did because we only had 3 shingles left when we finished.  From Bruce’s measurements I had figured it would take a total of 11 square but I was closer to 13 square.  I am not sure whose fault it was on the figuring but I am sure glad Mae was able to bring the other up to us.  Micheline had lunch ready when we finished the roof.  I repaired some of the Sufis on the back eves before quitting.   It was 3 PM when I headed for home.  I had to stop in Logan and get natural gas for the pickup.  Regular gas is too expensive when I can use natural gas.  It was good to get home.  We ordered Chinese food to be delivered and we relaxed and watched a couple of shows on TV.

 

Sunday, September 04, 2005

 

                We slept in until 7 AM and so had to hurry a little to get to Church on time.  I had filled out our donation slip and in the hurry had made an error.  Bother Rogers called to see what I wanted to do with the $30 extra so we put it into Humanitarian Aid for the hurricane relief.  We had enough Chinese food left over that we were able to have it this evening.  Bob came home after he returned and told me of the experience he had with Mark’s driving.  It was really quite funny as I had just told Mae that I was glad that I didn’t go with them as I was sure Bob would be upset with Mark and his driving.  It has sure been nice to relax today.  We started to bed fairly but then we rubbed each others feet and talked for a while.  It was almost 11 PM when we went to sleep.

 

Monday, September 05, 2005

 

                We got up at 6 AM and started getting ready to have our family come.  I cooked us an egg omelet for breakfast.  Both Mae and I worked all day getting ready for our family to come.  I burned all the branches that were left out back and got a nice fire pit ready to use to Dutch oven chicken and vegetables.  I had mowed part of the back yard before starting the fire.  Bob came out and finished mowing the back lawn.  About noon I put three Dutch Ovens on to cook.  We had corn on the cob.  Sandi came and got a peach cobbler ready to cook.  By 2:30 PM most of our family had arrived and we started to eat.  We had plenty for everyone.  We had Bob, Carroll and Mark come and eat with us.  Nick didn’t come with Sandi as he was working but he came later.  Jeff and Gail came late as they were with Gail’s family this morning.  With the exception of Shellie’s family we had all of our family here.  After eating we visited and played games.  Some of us played Horse Shoes, some played Frisbee and others played volleyball.  I guess it was around 6 PM when all of them had left and then Mae and I had some cleanup to do.  We were sure tired when we finished.  We rubbed each others feet.  I fell asleep in the chair for a while.  We took hot bath to relieve some of the back pain and applied Thera-gesic. 

 

                We have decided to send Roger Sylvia the $300 dollars that he feels he needs.  This is to be a loan, we will see.  We received a letter from Loreta P. Emdrina from the Guimba 3rd Branch.  We think she might be the President of the Primary.  She has a sad story and would like financial aid.  There are so many and how or who do you help?

It was quite chilly this morning and you can feel Fall coming.  Mae had breakfast ready when I returned home.  We then attended the regular Sunday Meetings.  Bob and Carroll invited us for dinner after meetings so I ate little as we had invited Jeff, Gail and Marcie for dinner at 5 PM.  I took a nap later.  Shortly before 5 PM Jeff called and said that they were trying to get Gail into a Doctor as she is in quite a bit of pain.  They weren’t able to come to dinner.  We put things up and warmed up some stew.  David invited us to come up this evening so we went up and played games and visited.  We got home at 9:30 PM and called Jeff.  They were just leaving the hospital.  Gail has a tubular pregnancy and she will have to go into the Dr. tomorrow and have it checked and probably aborted.
