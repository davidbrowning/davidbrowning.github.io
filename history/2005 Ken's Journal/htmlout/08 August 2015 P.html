                Although we were both tired and would like to have slept in, we got up.  After breakfast I went over to Garf's and installed a second medicine cabinet.  Mae went with Irven and Sandy to Orem to hear Rand Paul who is running for Presidential Nominee.  She felt quite good about him as a candidate.  It took me 5 hours to complete the job and they paid me before I left.  Both cabinets were quite a job as there was plumbing in the walls that took some modification to get the cabinets to fit.  After getting home, I lay down and slept for a ½ hour.  I then showered and shaved.  Mae also took a short nap and then she entered my diary entries that I had put in a note book while we were on our Reunion and Fishing trip.  After dinner we attended our Saturday Evening session of our Stake Conference.  It was very good.  After the Conference session, I took Mae down to Ogden looking for a ice cream store that served frozen yogurt.  We stopped at one shop but they were out of frozen yogurt.  We then found a Farr's Ice Cream store on Wall and Riverdale.  We had bought Mae a ice cream cone at the other store so I got a dish of yogurt and we came home.  Mae took a shower and we soon went to bed.

 

Sunday, August 30, 2015

 

                I woke at 5am but let Mae sleep until 6am before waking her up.  I got breakfast ready while Mae did her hair.  We left for Stake Conference shortly after 8am so that we could get a good parking spot as well as a good seat.  We did that, but shortly after the meeting started, I realized that the seat choice was a mistake as my left leg went into muscle cramps and I was unable to get out to walk the cramps off.  I suffered through the rest of the meeting.  When we got to the car, Mae had some pills for me, but my leg continued to hurt for some time even after getting home.  After eating some lunch, I took a nap and it seemed to help.  I really had a hard time hearing any of the speakers, so that really took away from the meeting for me.  On the way home Mae dropped her cell phone under the car seat.  After getting home she searched for her phone but also found a part to our GPS that we had thought had been lost for some time. 

 

Monday, August 31. 2015

 

                I woke early but was able to go back to sleep, although I didn't sleep very well.  We did not want to get up when the alarm went off.  Mae got my breakfast and lunch ready while I said morning prayers and made the bed.  I went to work for Mrs. Heli Hunsaker repairing a home that she will rent out.  Work went well but I had some questions for her, but she didn't come back around before I left.  I went down to Randy's and put the trolling plate back on the boat.  Randy would like to go fishing back to Strawberry Reservoir tomorrow and suggested that we stay overnight.  As I thought about it, I think it will work out.  After getting home I visited with Mae about the idea and decided that we will try it.  I spent this evening getting ready to go.  I plan on working for Mrs Hunsaker for a while in the morning and then going to get Randy.  Mae has been busy helping me get ready to go.  She is sure a special wife and sweetheart.
