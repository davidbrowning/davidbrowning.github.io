                I slept well but Mae was awake quite a bit last night.  This morning Shellie had some ladies coming over to visit so we tried and so we tried to help her prepare for them.  When they did come I came in the office and worked on some of my journals and histories.  They were here for six hours.  Judie called us to let us know that she has been sick for several days and feels that we should not stop there on the way home.  I told her that we would check back in a day or two to see how she is doing.  We had a late dinner and just visited after for a while.  I played a couple of games on the computer and added to mu journal.  It late and I am ready to go to bed now.  Mae and I took showers before going to bed.

 

Wednesday, April 29, 2015

 

                After breakfast Mae, Shellie and I went over to the Phoenix Arizona Temple and went through a session.  It is a small temple but very beautiful.  As we were leaving the Temple I met two Missionaries.  I visited with them for a few minutes.  Both of them are about at the end of their mission calling.  One of them was from Omaha, Nebraska and the other was from Burley, Idaho.  They said that they are allowed to attend the Temple once every three months.  I was glad to hear that as when we finished our mission in the Philippines we were told that we couldn’t attend the temple as that was not our purpose for being there.  After the session I was hungry so I suggested that we stop and get something to eat.  Shellie drove almost back to the house before stopping at a salad buffet “Sweet Tomato”.  It was pretty good but that wasn’t quite what I had in mind.  This evening we met Bruce and Katie, Bruce daughter at “Whole Foods” where we had dinner and enjoyed visiting with them.  Michalinea was at an appointment she had so couldn’t be with us.  On the way home Mae and I stopped and got a Frozen Yogurt before coming home.  After returning to Roy and Shellie’s we watched the movie “Ice Castles II” which we found very enjoyable but it is almost a duplication of the original “Ice Castles”.  After it ended we got ready for bed.  We talked for a while, very concerned about Roy and Shellie’s relationship.  We see very many danger signs, but we do not know how to approach the concerns.  I have tried to council Shellie in the past but that hasn’t helped.  I tried to talk to Roy in the past but that certainly was not accepted but almost caused great problems.  The only thing I know to do is pray because all else seems to be out of my abilities.

 

Thursday, April 30, 2015

 

                I woke about 7AM still with great concerns for my family.  Almost every time Mae and I attend the Temple, we add our children to the Prayer Roll and hope for Heavens intervention in our family’s behalf.  Every day I seem to realize that I am less and less qualified to council others as to how Life works.  My greatest comfort comes through my testimony of the truthfulness of the Gospel of Jesus Christ and I pray continually that my faith in my Savior Jesus Christ will never falter.  I don’t see anywhere else to turn for help.  How other possibly continues with life without that faith, I do not comprehend.

                It is another beautiful day in Arizona.  As I set here at the table typing this entry I look out the kitchen window onto a beautifully landscaped yard and a beautiful lake, even though it is a manmade lake, the beauties of nature is breath taking.  It is certainly a wonder to me, at all God has done for us.  Jase and I went over to get water and ice for Shellie.  I also stopped at Chevron and filled up with gas so that we will be ready to go tomorrow.  I did take a nap today.  Mae, Shellie, Kevis and I played a game of Dominion and Kevis came in next to last because Shellie, Mae and I tied for last place.  I worked on a batch of indexing and finished it this evening.  For dinner this evening Nora, Garrett and Maggie came over for dinner with us.
