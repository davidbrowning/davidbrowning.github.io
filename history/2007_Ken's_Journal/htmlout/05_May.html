                I got about 6 AM and have been going all day.  I got the TV antenna wires reran so that all TV are receiving very good reception.  I also got a water line pulled from the basement to the garage so that I can install.  I had to go to Home Depot for some parts so I also stopped at Sportsman Warehouse for worms.  I did some repairs to the tent trailer and checked that everything with the trailer is working ok.  I got gas for the truck and some parts to hook up some lights on my truck.  The remainder of the evening I finished Jeff and Gail’s house plans and got a e-mail sent to Quad-Lock Building Systems.  It was mid-night before I finished and we got to bed.

 

Tuesday, May 29, 2007

 

                Being that it was so late when we went to bed, we slept in.  Mae gave me a haircut before I took my shower.  Bob prepared breakfast of biscuits and gravy for us.  We didn’t visit long but started packing.  It was 11:50 AM when we finished packing and left with the boat and trailer.  We stopped at Winegar’s for a few groceries and some ice.  We then went to Valley Home Medical where they checked my c-pack for me.  It seems to test out ok.  We stopped for some lunch at Wendy’s and then drove to East Canon reservoir.  We can only drive 40 to 45 MPH or the boat begins to sway but we weren’t in a hurry anyway.  When we got there we selected the camp site and set up the trailer.  We then decided to go down to the boat ramp and let Mae practice backing the boat in.  After three times and she did pretty good we decided to take a boat ride.  We road to the South end of the reservoir and back.  We then decided to fish for a little while.  We each had strikes and hooked some but would loose them before they got to the boat,  I switched to a dark gray and silver repalla and as we came into the boat dock I caught a nice rainbow trout.  As we got back to the camp sight Tina Linderman called just to talk to Mae.  She just needed someone to visit with.  I did some repair work on the tent trailer.  Mae prepared some dinner but it was so late when we ate that I had to sleep in the recliner.  We are sure glad to have a heater in the tent trailer as it is quite cooled outside tonight.

 

Wednesday, May 30, 2007

 

                We didn’t get up until late.  I cooked breakfast of fish, potatoes and eggs.  It was 11 PM when we ate.  Mae worked on her scrap books while I started a material take off list on Jeff and Gail’s addition.  Mae called Shellie and visited with her and the kids as the kids just returned from school.  Today was the end of school for this year.  Mae made us some lunch.  After eating we went fishing about 5PM.  We each had several strikes but I caught three.  It was 9:30 PM when we got back to camp.  We had a cup of hot Postum and ate some water melon while we played a game of Racko before we went to bed.  It has been a very enjoyable day even if Mae beat me in Racko.

 

Thursday, May 31, 2007

 

                I got up at 5 AM and went fishing.  Mae slept in and enjoyed a relaxing morning.  I caught 3 fish and let one go as it was to small.  We ate when I got back to camp.  I worked on the truck running an electric wire with a switch and a indicator light for extra backup lights on the pickup.  These will give me light when I am hooking up boats and trailers at night.  We went down the road and found a bush that I could cut winner sticks to use this evening.  I then shaved and showered and cleaned up.  Shauna and the kids came up this afternoon shortly after 5 PM.  We prepared and cooked hot dogs for dinner.  After cleaning up we took the boat out fishing.  Mae backed the boat and trailer down the ramp and did it perfectly.  We fished for quite a while but didn’t even get a bite this evening.  Connor had a small fishing pole to play with but he dropped it over the side of the boat and I sunk before we could retrieve it.  He felt bad but Shauna assured him that they would buy him another one.  When we went to load the boat Mae backed the trailer down the dock and did a pretty good job of it.  When we got to camp we made pop corn, hot chocolate and Postum which we ate and drank before we went to bed.
