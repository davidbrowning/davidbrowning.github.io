                I woke up at 4:15 AM with the start of a cold.  As the day went on my cold got worse.  I let Mae sleep until 6 AM and then we started packing.  It was 9 AM before we left the house.  We stopped for natural gas and found that the electrical adapter that I used on the trailer is not long enough and it comes unplugged when I turn a right turn.  I tried to repair it but finally called Bob and asked him to bring me the adapter from the boat trailer.  We had planned to visit Chelci and her new baby but with both Mae and I having colds we called her and said we would come and visit when we are feeling better.  We don’t want to expose Chelci or the baby to these colds.  We met Sandi in Tooele and went to her new home.  It is beautiful and large.  She would like me to come out and build a craft room.  I will have to do it when we get back from this trip.  When we got to Orem to get natural gas the pump wasn’t working.  We had to go on to Springfield where we got gas.  We also stopped at Fillmore and Cedar City for natural gas.  Just as we were leaving the freeway to go to the park, Jackie Thomas called Mae and told her that the one secretary had fallen and broke her leg very seriously and would be off work for at least two months or longer.  they would like Mae to come back to work to fill in for her.  Mae will have to decide in the next two days.  It was almost 8 PM when we got to Zion National Park.  When we got our camp site we found that there are electrical hookups only.  That does present a problem but there is nothing that we can do about it now.  It was late when we got set up and something to eat.  After eating I worked on my journal while Mae did up the dishes.  We then had to play games for a while to let the dinner set before we could go to bed. 

 

Thursday, September 27, 2007

 

                I didn’t sleep very well and was awake early this morning.  I napped several times today.  It was relaxing to be here in Zion’s National Park.  Jeff and Gail got here about 3 PM.  This evening we went for a walk just up to the ranger gate and back.  after returning and putting Benjamin to bed, we enjoyed playing games. 

 

Friday, September 28, 2007

 

                Although I woke several times I was able to go right back to sleep.  We had a nice breakfast and then went for a hike up to Zion’s Arch.  It was mile round trip hike.  We sure enjoyed it.  We came back and relaxed at camp.  About 5 PM we went into town and did a little shopping.  We bought some ice cream and they also had some frozen yogurt for me.  Jeff and Gail’s friend, Stewart, Neisha and their three children Kyler, Adderly and Mckye came this evening.  They are in a tent camping area but we are inviting them to eat and spend time with us.  This evening we fried hamburgers and roasted hot dogs.  Mae of course had all the trimming and salad to with it.  The Baur’s had reservations for a tent area and so was some distance from us.  We discussed going on a hike in the morning and finally Jeff and I decided to hike Angels Landing.  Mae was quite upset that I would try it as I haven’t felt well for the past three days but we decided to do it.  We prepared before going to bed to leave at 7 AM.

 

Saturday, September 29, 2007

 

                I woke at 5:30 AM and went over to the bathroom.  I got me some breakfast and woke Jeff.  It was just after 7 AM when we left.  Mae did get up and help me get off.  It was a beautiful morning and we enjoyed the hike.  It was a very enjoyable hike and I was surprised how fast we made it to the top pf the switch backs.  It was only another ½ mile to the top but it was the slowest part of the trip.  We sure enjoyed the view and the feeling that we had made it there.  We stayed for sometime there at the top and ate some snacks and visited.  As we returned we found several people that gave up and returned with out going all the way.  We returned to camp about 12:30 PM so we made good time.  I took a nap after we ate some lunch.  The Baur’s came over and played games.  I took a nap.  This afternoon Jeff, Gail, Benji, Mae and the Baur’s all went up to the river trail.  I stayed in camp to cook the Dutch oven dinner for tonight.  I did go to town and get Jeff some Lime Diet Coke and some ice.  I also bought me another frozen yogurt.  I had trouble getting the fire hot enough to cook on but I had dinner ready before Mae and all of them returned.  It cooled off quite a lot this evening.  We sure enjoy having the heater in the trailer.  Mae and Gail went to Springdale and paid to take showers.  They brought me a token for tomorrow but it was to late this evening.

 

Sunday, September 30, 2007

 

                We got up and made breakfast.  The Baur’s came over for breakfast and then thanked us for being able to come down with us.  They went back to their camp and started packing to go home.  I took a shower in the camp trailer.  Gail, Benji, Mae and I attended Sacrament meeting in Springdale.  While we were gone Jeff filled the water tank, did dishes and packed their things so they were about ready to leave for home.  We ate the rest of the Dutch oven dinner left over from last night.  After eating Jeff, Gail and Benji soon left for home.  I took a nap and Mae read.  Mae and I then packed us a lunch and we went on the Park Shuttle to the Narrows and took the River Walk.  We got off the shuttle where we could see Angles Landing and I explained where Jeff and I hiked yesterday.  We then caught the next shuttle and road on up to the Narrows.  We hiked the trail to the nerror’s and returned back to the shuttle.  It was dark when we got back to our trailer so we soon went to bed.  It is sure nice to be alone with my sweetheart.
