Tuesday, July 31, 2012

 

                We got up early and worked most of the day getting ready for the reunion.  I did go with Brother Morgan to check on meat prices for the Ward Social.  We also went to the Chapel on 800 N and checked out the bowery.  It is very nice but the afternoon sun will shine into the west end.  Maybe we can get permission to hang a tarp on that end.  When I went out to gather the eggs this evening I found our white chicken coming out from behind a box in the shed.  When I pulled the box out I found where the chickens have been laying the past few days.  There was 14 eggs in that nest, 1 broken one and evident that there had been another that the chickens had eaten.  We worked until 7:30PM before we quite.  We watched the movie “Hunt for Red October” on DVD.  Although we have seen it before and it has some rough language, we quite enjoyed it.
