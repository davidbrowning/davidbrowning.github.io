                Mae was sleeping in the recliner when my alarm went off.  I tried not to wake her but she woke up and got my breakfast ready.  My morning run went well.  When I got I took care of the animals and then went to the post office where I mailed some pictures to Roy and Linda.  I then went to Best Buy where I got some ink cartridges for our printer and some canned air to clean the key board.  I took a short nap and then Scott came over to have lunch with us.  During lunch we got talking to Scott about the Church and we just upset him.  I guess that he feels that I yelled at him.  I didn’t think I did but I am not the best communicator so maybe he took it that way.  I do not understand how he is thinking.  My afternoon run went well.  The regular driver of the route called in and requested that she not come back until Monday.  I called Roberta and volunteered to continue driving the route this week and she could get someone else to drive the field trips.  After dinner tonight I entered some of my payroll and bid on some more field trips for next week.  Brother John Gallegos came over this evening.  Mae was able to help him with his Family Search.  She was able to show him how to add information and then he was able to find one person that he now had enough information to take to the Temple.  Mae is getting very good at this calling.  We went to bed quite concerned about our three sons.
