Monday, October 01, 2012

 

                We got up this morning and cooked fish and hash browns (sweet potatoes, onions, green peppers and eggs) and toast on the large grill.  After breakfast we cleaned up and got everything packed to go home.  After the camp was ready we went over to get the boat.  The store, cabins and boat dock closed yesterday at noon.  We were told that we could still use the ramp to get our boat our today.  The boat trailer was parked in front of the cabins and that area was blocked off until we had to pull the trailer to the road to hook it onto the pickup.  We then went out fishing for a couple of hours and caught five nice fish.  At two PM we quite fishing and loaded the boat and headed for home.  Mae and I took the lead as we left and so I had Mae put the GPS on.  Following the GPS it took us a different way home and at first we were concerned about following it but finally determined we would.  It took us a better and shorter way home getting us there at 6:30PM even with a stop to get fried chicken.  We got unpacked and then went to bed.
