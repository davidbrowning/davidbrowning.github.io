Friday, May 30, 2014

Saturday, May 31, 2014

 

                We left St. George at 5AM driving to Las Vegas where we stopped at Denny’s and had breakfast.  We traveled on to South Valley Farms in Wasco California, getting there about 12:30PM.  We enjoyed the dinner and visiting with many from the farm.  We had a good visit with Lee and Kim.  We then went sightseeing around the farm and especially over to our home in Shafter.  There have been many changes in the farm since we were there.  The home on River Side has been completely renovated, stuccoed  and re-landscaped.  Also they have built a double garage for it along with a second home there.  We had some time so we went to the park and parked in the shade and took a nap.  We got a call from Ken and Vivian Shaw letting us know that they had arrived.  We went to the motel and visited with them for a while.  We then went to the Wasco Ward social where Kim and Lee were again honored prior to Lee being released as Bishop tomorrow.  We left there and drove to Roy and Linda’s in Fresno.  We visited with them and stayed there tonight.
