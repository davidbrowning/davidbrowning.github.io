                I got up at 5 AM but then went back to bed until 8 AM.  I am still not feeling well so did very little.  Most of the day I spent in bed resting or sleeping.  Mae went to town about 3 pm and didn’t return until 6 PM.  I was just thinking that I might call her to see where she was, when she came in to check on me.  We thought about watching a show on TV but couldn’t find any thing worth watching.  I fell asleep while Mae got on the inter-net.  We read another chapter of our book before going to bed.

 

Tuesday, January 31, 2006

 

                We slept in and Mae got up about 8 AM but I slept most of the morning.  I did get a little work done on the computer this afternoon.  Jeff called and wanted Mae and I to go look at a couple of homes with them.  I felt much better so we went with them.  They looked at one just two or three blocks above us and one in Clearfield.  I liked the one in Clinton best but they are asking $135,000 for it.  That is a lot of money.  This evening I met with Steve Smith and Mark McPhee as a High Priest Leadership.  Scott was out of town so didn’t meet with us tonight.  After returning home I checked with the Brethren under my Home Teaching supervision as to the home teaching for this month.  It looks pretty good.  I called and talked to Lois to ask them if they could sew a quilt for us for Chelci’s wedding.  We will have to call them back with the colors when we find that out from Chelci.  We read two more chapters from our book before I did my journal and we went to bed.
