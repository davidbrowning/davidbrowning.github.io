                I woke during the early morning hours and couldn’t go back to sleep so got up and lay in the recliner.  I slept fairly well but thought of Craig and Carla’s job.  with the change off of day light saving time we got an extra hour this morning.  After breakfast I got a short nap.  We got a new Bishopric to day.  Brother Steve Smith was called to be Bishop.  He chose Brother Mark McPhie and Brother Thurman as counselors.  We don’t know Brother Thurman as he has been a clerk in the Singles Ward.  After meeting we went down to the LDS Hospital in SL and saw Sister Robin Walker.  She was involved in a auto accident last Tuesday and will be recovering for some time.  This evening we went to Jeff and Gail’s for dinner and played games after.  It was 10:30 PM when we got home.  I made a call to Craig and Carla and to Randy after getting home.  Well I am tired so will call this good for tonight.

 

Monday, October 30, 2006

 

                We got up early and was down to Randy’s shortly after 8 AM.  It was almost 11 am when we got to the cabin.  I started right to work and found that the door was wrong, it was 79” over all and I needed 76”.  We decided to do all other items and then take the door back tomorrow.  I worked until 6 PM when I stopped for dinner and went right back to work.  I finished and loaded every thing up.  We left for home at 9:17 PM and got home at 11:15 PM.  I was so tired that I went right to bed and fell asleep almost as soon as my head rested on the pillow.

 

Tuesday, October 31, 2006

 

                We slept in and when we did wake up we stayed in bed longer.  I tried to get reservations at Strawberry Reservoir lodge for Friday night but they are closed for the season.  I was able to get reservation at Daniel’s Summit.  I had several things to go to town after so this afternoon was spent chasing.  Mae also did some chasing this afternoon.  This evening Mae went over to Jeff and Gail’s and spent the evening with them.  I went to help Scott run the wires to his speaker but a lot of his time was spent in answering the door for Trick-or-Treaters.  I got home before Mae and took a shower to clean the saw dust and insulation off.  When Mae got home we tried to clean the DVD player but it still doesn’t work.  We started to watch Perry Mason but both of us fell asleep.  We didn’t get any Trick-or-Treaters at our place as we are in back.  Even Bob & Carroll only get a few as most of the children stay in the sub-divisions where there are more homes.
