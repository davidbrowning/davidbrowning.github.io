Madsen and watched that.  I fell asleep at the end of the program and Mae let me sleep.  We visit with David’s family and Sandi by phone.  David said that he is still having trouble with his leg.  He feels that the Doctor wrapped his leg to tight and it shut the circulation of and damaged the nerves.  David is afraid that he may have problems the rest of his life from it.  Sandi and Nick are having difficulty over the pup that Nick has.  It is sure hard to know that your children are having problems and there isn’t much you can do except pray for them.  Mae prepared us some fruit and toast and then I added to the monthly report.  I re-watched the last of the Truman Madsen CD that I slept through.  It was only a few minutes of it.  That has been a very interesting and informative series to play.  Mae worked more on the report while I got ready for bed.  After going to bed we talked for a while about our children, wondering what we might do to help them.  Mae said that David had told her about a book they had read that described the possible way that with the modern tech knowledge this nation could be destroyed.  It doesn’t sound very far off from what I am afraid is coming.  I just wish David could realize that scriptures has warned us of this for a long time.

 

Monday, September 27, 2010

Shafter, California

 

                I woke late as I had set the clock wrong.  Mae didn’t sleep very well last night so I let her sleep in and got myself off to work.  Mae had prepared my lunch and all I had to do was to put it in my lunch box.  She is sure a special wife and sweetheart.  Elder Shaw and I work all day still preparing for painting.  I stopped on the way home and got some cool whip for tonight as we are having Family Home Evening at our place.  I showered and shaved and by the time I finished Mae had dinner ready.  After dinner I took a nap.  We enjoyed having the Shaw’s here this evening.  I made this entry and then we went to bed.

 

Tuesday, September 28, 2010

Shafter, California

 

                I woke a little before the alarm went off so I shut it off and set in the recliner.  Mae soon woke and realized that I wasn’t in bed so she got up to see where I was.  By then it was time to get up anyway.  I went to work and Mae and Sister Shaw went to Bakersfield shopping.  While there Mae bought us a new camera.  Lee came by this morning and gave Elder Shaw and me a hand with the masking.  I had already got all the doors washed and ready to paint.   After lunch we started painting inside the house and got the master bedroom, a bathroom and the laundry room painted before we quite for the day.  Mae got home just before I did so I helped carry things into the house.  While she was finishing up dinner I showered and shaved.  Mike called us just to talk for a few minutes.  It was good to talk to him.  The Elders came for dinner this evening and we always enjoy having them.  I helped Mae do the dishes and then worked on this entry.  We were tired but at 8:30PM we decided to watch one of the “Big Valley” series.  I finished this entry and we went to bed.

 

Wednesday, September 29, 2010

Shafter, California

 

                Although I set the alarm for 6AM, I woke at 5AM and couldn’t go back to sleep so stayed up.  Mae soon got up and felt that she needed to get started as she is working at the grade school today instead of Friday.  She made pancakes for breakfast and they were very good.  I helped Mae with the dishes before I left for work.  Elder Shaw and I finished the hand painting and then started on the spray painting.  It wasn’t long when we realized that we didn’t have enough paint so elder Shaw went to home depot to get another 5 gallons.  I continued to paint and was afraid that at the rate of paint usage that we might still be short.  I called Home Depot and talked to the Paint Department and told them that Elder Shaw was coming for the 5 gallons of paint but to have him get 10 gallons instead.  My thoughts were that if he brought 10 then we were safe and it we didn’t need it now we could use it on the home and duplex.  When Elder Shaw got back we finished the inside and then painted the doors.  We finished the doors and then had to clean up the paint pump.  It was 4:45PM when we finished and went home, both of us were very tired.  Mae had dinner about ready when I got home so I just washed up the best I could.  My hands we badly covered with paint.  We put on a movie to watch while we ate our dinner.  It was a different movie but an oldie.  I showered and shaved and spent quite a while scrubbing my hands.  I made this entry and we read some of the instructions on our new camera before we went to bed. 

 

Thursday, September 30, 2010

Shafter, California

 

                I woke early and slept in the recliner for a while.  After breakfast I worked on my financial report and am missing one receipt.  Mae and I had an appointment to meet with Merrill Dibble this morning at 9AM.  He visited with us for about an hour.  Mae came back home and I went to the Kimberlina home.  Elder Shaw and I removed all the taping and gathered up all the trash.  Elder Shaw left at noon as they are going to San Diego for the weekend.  I emptied the trash into the dumpster along with cleaning up some other things.  I brought all the doors in side and stood them up to finish drying.  I didn’t want them left outside over the weekend.  I came home a few minutes after 2PM and on the way home it rained.  This is the first rain we have seen since the end of March.  After showering and shaving I made some almond butter.  Mae had most things packed so we left for Fresno before 4PM.  When we got to Fresno I installed a new toilet for Roy and Linda.  When I started to fill the water closet we found that it was cracked.  Roy will have to take it back for replacement.  It was after 10PM when we got to bed.
