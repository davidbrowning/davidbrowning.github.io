Shellie called and invited us to go to Israel on a tour with them in March.  It would cost $7,000 for the two of us to go for 11 days.  We have discussed it throw the day and I just don’t think we can afford to do it.  Bob came down a little later and said he had waffles ready.  I got up for that.  We had trouble with the electrical circuit breaking the tripping.  I didn’t feel very good so laid down in the recliner and slept for a while.  I got up about noon and started drawing house plans for Randy and Judy.  Bob made home made bread and gave us a loaf.  We had turkey stew and bread for super.  Mae and I went to a special fireside that the Stake Presidency asked us to attend.  It was on finances and budgeting.  It was good to review that information.  We got home at 9 PM.  I wanted to watch a show but decided to check out our bank statement on Quicken.  Something is wrong with our balance and we spent some time trying to detect the problem but gave up for this evening.

 

Saturday, January 27, 2007

 

                I woke at 4:30 AM and was up for a while in the bathroom and reading the scriptures.  I went into the front room, spent sometime on my knees in prayer; I then used the recliner to rest as I was afraid of waking Mae if I went back to bed.  The alarm went of at 6:30 AM and woke Mae.  We lay back down for about ½ hour before getting up.  We went up to David’s for breakfast of biscuits and gravy.  David prepared breakfast and it was very good.  At 10 AM we went over to Morgan’s elementary school and watched Tyler play basketball.  Tyler is pretty good but it is amazing at the skills some of these boys have at 8 years old.  The one area that was amusing was the referees didn’t call for double dribbling.  We visited with David for a few minutes after going back to their home.  Mae and I then came home.  Mae got ready and went to the Temple.  I worked the rest of the day on our Quicken file getting it restored.  At 5 PM Mark and I went over to Irven and Sandy’s for dinner.  Mae met us there as she was late getting out of the Temple session.  For desert Sandy had bought me a ½ gallon of Maverick frozen yogurt.  We then went to the Layton City building where we attended the first of three classes on the Constitution.  The class lasted three hours but kept me totally interested.  I am also disturbed at some of the things that were presented.  It was after 10:30 PM when we got home.  I entered this journal entry before going to bed.

 

Sunday, January 28, 2007

 

                We got up early and went to the Sacrament of the Country Creek Ward at 9 AM.  After meeting we went over to Scott and Mishelle’s and left Scott’s Birthday present.  We then went over to Tina Linderman’s where I gave her a Priesthood blessing.  We visited with her for a little while and then came on down to Mike and Becky’s.  We got there before their meetings were out but Becky had stayed home with the little girls.  Mike and Becky soon left for St. George for a conference that Mike is to attend.  We held F.H.E. this afternoon so that we could have an activity with the children tomorrow evening.  This evening I watched the movie “It’s a Wonderful Life” on TV in Mike and Becky’s bedroom.  I stopped for Family Prayer and then played a game of Rummy with Tayler and David.  I then finished my movie.

 

Monday, January 29, 2007

 

                Mae got up and got breakfast for the children this morning.  I slept in until just before 8 AM.  After breakfast I worked on a budget and did so until 5 PM.  Mae warmed up some turkey stew and made scones for diner.  We took the kids bowling but had to wait for a lane for almost an hour.  We all had a good time.  It was late when we got home so the children went right to bed.  I caught my journal up to date before taking a shower and going to bed also.

 

Tuesday, January 30, 2007

 

                I got up at 6 AM and after being up for a hour I went back to bed.  Mae got the kids off to school and Mattie off to pre-school but she let me sleep until 9:45 AM.  I fixed my own breakfast and then started working on the Porter’s house plans.  I had to do some changes to the layer layout that I had.  That took me over and hour.  I worked a little over 3 hours on them but then had to quite so we could go to a basketball game that David played in.  It was a good game and David played quite well.  If you ask his grandparents he did exultant but them they have no favorites.  We stopped at a grocery store on the way home for a few things.  Mae soon had dinner ready when we got home.  I gave her a helping hand.  David and Taylor got ready and left for Mutural.  Taylor made it there but David got side tracked somewhere.  After they got home and Mae had the little girls to bed, we played some games.  After Taylor went to bed David and I got talking about religion.  Mae finally said I had to let David get to bed for school in the morning.  We sure have some great grandchildren.  I filled in my journal before going to bed.

 

Wednesday, January 31, 2007

 

                I got up earlier this morning and had breakfast about 7:30 AM.  I did some work on the plans through the day but we got ready to go home.  We took the little girls over to Becky’s sister about 11 AM.  We were going to visit Pat but Mae called her and she is recovering from a fall.  She didn’t feel like company today.  We got home about 1 PM.  After eating some lunch I lay down for a few minutes.  I asked Mae to wake me in 20 minutes but 50 minutes later Bob came down and woke me.  Mae had got talking to Georgia and forgot me.  We called the sleep center and got me an appointment for a c-pack test Sunday evening.  I went to the Credit Union and opened an account for my Handyman work.  I got a bank check for the cost of the tickets for the Jerusalem tour trip and mailed it.  I chased around trying to find 200 clean, crisp one dollar bills for us to take on the trip.  I was able to get about 100 and the Clearfield and Wingers Cr. Union are going to try to save me some one dollar bills this next week.  Mae fixed me some dinner but she didn’t eat before going to a meeting on Visiting Teaching.  She is to do a workshop during the meeting.  When she got home she said that there weren’t very many sisters that attended.  I got a little more drawn on the plans but I stopped and watched the movie “The March of the Penguins”.  I finished my journal and went to bed about 10:30 PM.
